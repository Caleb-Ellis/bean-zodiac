Spicy is not one flavour but many — the bright hit of fresh chilli, the slow build of black pepper, the nasal flare of wasabi, the lingering warmth of ginger — but all share the same quality: they demand your full attention. Now. You cannot eat something spicy while distracted. A Spicy Year has this same commanding, undeniable presence.

Spicy Years burn away hesitation and clear the palate of everything stale. The discomfort is not incidental — it is the mechanism. The heat, the flushed face, the slight fear before you commit: these are signs that something real is happening. Spicy Years are the ones you remember.

Spicy's shadow is overwhelm. Heat without restraint only burns. The Spicy Year asks for boldness with intention — not intensity for its own sake.
