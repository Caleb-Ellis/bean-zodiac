Spicy is not one Flavour but many — the bright hit of fresh chilli, the slow build of black pepper, the nasal flare of wasabi, the lingering warmth of ginger — but all share the same fundamental quality: they demand your full attention. You cannot eat something spicy while distracted. A spicy year has this same commanding presence.

The Spicy Year is a year for bold action. They burn away hesitation, clear the palate of everything stale, and create the kind of intensity that is unforgettable in retrospect. The discomfort is the point. The heat, the flushed face, the eyes watering — these are signs that something real is happening, that you are genuinely present in your own life.

Spicy's shadow is aggression. Heat without context scorches rather than warms. The Spicy Year asks for intensity with intention — not heat for its own sake, but fire directed toward something worth building.
