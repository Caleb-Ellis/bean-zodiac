Sweet is the easiest flavour to love and the hardest to use well. Too much and everything becomes the same note — cloying, flat, numbing. In the right amount, sweetness is generosity made edible: it opens, softens, and invites. Sweet Phases have this same quality of uncomplicated welcome.

Sweet Phases carry effortlessness. Connections form naturally. Goodwill circulates freely. The world feels gentler than it usually does, and this is not luck — it is the year extending an invitation. The only task is to receive what is given, and to extend the same outward.

The Sweet Phase's shadow is complacency. Ease sustained without intention curdles into avoidance. The Sweet Phase asks you to savour abundance without mistaking it for the permanent order of things.
