Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most rational — not cold-blooded, but constitutionally unable to proceed on grounds they cannot account for.

Edamame strips away the unnecessary; Bitter withholds assent until something has genuinely earned it; Fermented means the reasoning runs deeper than the surface, turning over premises others don't examine. These Beans reach the right answer by a route that can be shown, step by step, to anyone willing to walk it.

The Bitter Fermented Edamame's challenge is that other people aren't always willing. The well-reasoned conclusion that arrives without warmth often fails to change anything. These Beans must remember that logic is the engine, not the vehicle.
