Edamame strips away the unnecessary; Bitter Beans withhold assent until something earns it; Fermented Beans turn over premises others don't examine.

The Fermented Bitter Edamame's challenge is that Beans aren't always willing. Well-reasoned conclusions without warmth fail to change anything. They must remember logic is the engine, not the vehicle.
