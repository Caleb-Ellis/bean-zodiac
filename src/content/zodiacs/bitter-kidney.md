People born in the Year of the Bitter Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most devoted — passionate, protective, and equipped with a Bitter discernment that makes them choose carefully who and what receives that intensity. When they give their loyalty, it is total and genuine and essentially permanent. Being chosen by a Bitter Kidney Bean is one of the zodiac's significant honours.

The Bitter Kidney Bean's challenge is letting people close enough to be chosen — the selectivity that makes their loyalty valuable can also keep it permanently out of reach.
