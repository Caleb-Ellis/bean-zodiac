People born in the Year of the Bitter Kidney Bean are the passionate, vital people who have discovered the surprising power of restraint. They have the Kidney Bean's considerable strength and deep earthiness alongside a Bitter patience that keeps asking their energy to slow down and take the long route. They are not naturally slow people. They are people who have learned, through some effort, that their vitality is most impressive when it has somewhere to rest — that the pot left to cook all afternoon is more nourishing than anything done in a hurry. Their endurance is one of their greatest qualities, and they are still figuring out how impressive it is.
