Dehydrated Butter Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most chastened — the ease is gone, the comfort has been questioned, and what remains is more careful.

Butter Beans trust warmth and slow time; Sour Beans cut through comfort with precision; Dried Beans remove what warmth was left, leaving a Bean that knows better now.

The Dehydrated Butter Bean's challenge is excessive caution born of experience. Not every situation that resembles the last one is the last one. These Beans must remember that caution earned is not the same as comfort abandoned.
