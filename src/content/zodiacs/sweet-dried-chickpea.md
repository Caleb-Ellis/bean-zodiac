Dried Beans born in the Year of the Sweet Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most wholesome — they have been everywhere, and they carry the warmth of every room they've passed through.

Chickpeas bridge worlds and carry warmth; Sweet Beans extend comfort without reservation; Dried Beans hold it in concentrated form, portable and sustaining.

The Dried Sweet Chickpea's challenge is depth beneath the goodwill. Warmth freely given is a gift — but a Bean known only for being warm is not always fully seen. These Beans must allow the complexity they carry to also be part of what they offer.
