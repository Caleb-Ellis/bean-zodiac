People born in the Year of the Sour Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most clear-eyed — courageous by instinct, but equipped with a Sour honesty that strips away any comforting illusions about what the leap will actually involve.

They go anyway. This is what distinguishes their courage from recklessness: not absence of knowledge, but a full accounting of it, followed by a deliberate yes.

The Sour Fava Bean's challenge is not letting full knowledge of what a leap costs become a reason to make the accounting one more time.
