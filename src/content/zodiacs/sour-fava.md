They have the Fava Bean's historical weight and quiet authority, but the Sour Flavour lifts that weight just enough to make it applicable rather than heavy. They are the people who know exactly what to do in a situation that has no obvious precedent, because they recognise it from something older. Their wisdom is not decorative. It is working.
