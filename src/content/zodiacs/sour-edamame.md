---
bean: edamame
flavour: sour
dish: Edamame with citrus salt. The snack that finally knows what it is.
fortune: Potential without direction is just possibility — name where yours is going, pick a door, and walk through it. The door does not care if you are certain.
---

People born in the Year of the Sour Edamame are the bright, energetic souls who are in the excellent and slightly uncomfortable process of becoming intentional. They have the Edamame's genuine energy and gift for freshness, but the Sour flavour in their character keeps asking the friendly and specific question: which direction, exactly? They are not short of ability or enthusiasm. They are learning that the decision about where to aim those gifts is itself a form of power — and that choosing one door and walking through it is not a betrayal of all the other doors. It is the beginning of a story.
