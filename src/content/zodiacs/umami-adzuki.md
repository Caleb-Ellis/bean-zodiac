People born in the Year of the Umami Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most memorable — warm and genuinely joyful, but with an Umami depth that makes their celebrations feel earned and lasting. They do not throw parties; they build feasts. People in their orbit often describe certain evenings in their company as among the best they have had, without being entirely able to explain why. The answer is that they did not skip steps.

The Umami Adzuki Bean's challenge is being present at the feast they have spent so long building — not just the last one in the kitchen.
