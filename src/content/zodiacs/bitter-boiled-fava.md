Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Boiled Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most courageous — they do not court the easy thing. Where others weigh the risk, these Beans have already started moving.

Fava Bean knows that what is worth having requires the double preparation; Bitter means that standard is not abandoned — the courageous act must genuinely earn its name; Boiled gives it patience, the long steady hold that makes courage a practice rather than a single moment. These Beans do not perform bravery for an audience. They simply continue.

The Bitter Boiled Fava Bean's challenge is knowing that not every difficult thing deserves doing. Courage applied without discernment is just endurance. These Beans must occasionally ask whether what they are facing down is worth facing.
