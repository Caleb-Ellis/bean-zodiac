Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most translucent — their care passes through without casting a shadow, which means others look through them to what is beyond.

Mung Beans heal without demanding anything in return; Sweet Beans make that care feel like something naturally given; Smoked Beans make the source barely visible even as its effects are real.

The Smoked Sweet Mung Bean's challenge is opacity. These Beans must occasionally be the thing that is seen, not the medium through which something else is seen.
