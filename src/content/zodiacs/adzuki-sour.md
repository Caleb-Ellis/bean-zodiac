tagline: What sourness does to sweetness - it makes it honest rather than cloying.

The reason a really good dessert has a sour element — citrus, a fermented cream, something sharp alongside the sweet — is that it makes the joy more specific and therefore more real. Sour does this for your year: honest questions arrive in the middle of the good times, and they make everything better rather than worse. Your joy doesn't disappear under scrutiny. It just becomes the specific, genuine version rather than the general festive one. The honest version of the celebration turns out to be the best version.

**Fortune for this year:** The honest version of the celebration is the best version.
