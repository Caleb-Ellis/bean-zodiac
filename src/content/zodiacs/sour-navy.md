People born in the Year of the Sour Navy Bean are the steady, community-minded souls who are in the process of becoming intentional about their loyalties. They have the Navy Bean's foundational dependability and deep commitment to the people around them, sharpened by a Sour clarity that wants to know the specific reason for each connection rather than simply maintaining all of them by habit. They are excellent at showing up. They are learning to ask why they are showing up, which makes the showing-up mean considerably more. The relationships they choose to deepen after this examination are the ones they will keep for life.
