People born in the Year of the Sweet Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most resourceful — practical, direct, and in a Sweet Year, finding that their pragmatic instincts meet almost nothing in the way.

They have the Edamame's quality of showing up fully and becoming exactly what's needed, and the sweetness in their character means that what's needed tends to align with what they have. They do not fully trust this yet.

The Sweet Edamame's challenge is trusting the year that asks nothing hard of them — the easy path is still the right one.
