Dried Beans born in the Year of the Bitter Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most clinical — they see what is depleted with precision and apply exactly what is needed, no more.

Mung Beans tend what is broken; Bitter Beans require that care be earned and precise; Dried Beans remove the gentleness from the instinct, leaving only the accuracy.

The Dried Bitter Mung Bean's challenge is the warmth stripped out. Care without feeling can do much of the work — but not all of it. These Beans must occasionally let the people they restore know that they are also cared for.
