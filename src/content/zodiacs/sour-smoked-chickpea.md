Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Sour Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most wary — they have been everywhere, and they remember clearly what they found.

Chickpeas fit every room without committing to any of them; Sour Beans record what they observe without softening it; Smoked Beans carry that record from somewhere that cannot be read directly.

The Smoked Sour Chickpea's challenge is openness. Wariness is hard-won, but so is a wall. These Beans must find a room worth committing to.
