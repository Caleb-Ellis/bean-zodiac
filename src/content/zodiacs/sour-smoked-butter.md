Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Sour Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most pallid — the calm is present, but it has been drained of something that made it sustaining.

Butter Beans offer ease as a natural condition; Sour Beans cut through comfort to what remains; Smoked Beans give the combination a quality that is difficult to name but hard to be near for long.

The Smoked Sour Butter Bean's challenge is warmth. These Beans must find their way back to something replenishing — not just the place where nothing demands anything.
