Ashen Chickpeas are the Bean Zodiac's most liminal — always between places, never quite committed to any of them.

Chickpeas are at home in every room without belonging to any; Bitter Beans resist the obvious arrival; Smoked Beans make the location of whoever they are impossible to confirm.

The Ashen Chickpea's challenge is arrival. Moving between rooms is not the same as being present in one. These Beans must eventually close a door behind them.
