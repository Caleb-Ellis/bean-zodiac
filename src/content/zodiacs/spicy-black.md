People born in the Year of the Spicy Black Bean are the slow-burn revelation — quiet for longer than seems possible, and then suddenly, unmistakably, the most interesting person in the room. They carry the Black Bean's depth and self-containment alongside a heat that surprises everyone, including sometimes themselves. Their intensity is real; they simply prefer to let it out when it matters rather than dispersing it for general warmth. When they finally commit to something fully — a project, a person, a position — the force of it is considerable. They were never just an ingredient. They were always the whole dish.
