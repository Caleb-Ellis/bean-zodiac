People born in the Year of the Spicy Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most surprising — quiet for longer than seems possible, and then suddenly, unmistakably, the most compelling presence in the room.

They carry the Black Bean's depth alongside a heat that surprises everyone, including sometimes themselves. When they finally commit fully, the force of it is considerable.

The Spicy Black Bean's challenge is not letting the quiet period run so long that the heat has nowhere left to go.
