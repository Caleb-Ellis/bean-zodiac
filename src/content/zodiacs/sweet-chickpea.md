People born in the Year of the Sweet Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most reliably helpful souls, which is genuinely a gift and occasionally their blind spot. They have the Chickpea's warmth and social ease in its most giving form, and the sweetness in their character makes them genuinely delightful to be around — not useful in the way of a tool, but welcome in the way of a good thing. Their complication is a deep habit of usefulness that sometimes prevents them from simply enjoying the room. They are learning, imperfectly, that sometimes the most generous thing they can do is let it be about them.
