People born in the Year of the Sweet Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most welcoming — warm, adaptable, and constitutionally inclined to make whoever is nearby feel at ease. The sweetness in their character makes their warmth land as delight rather than as effort.

The Sweet Chickpea's challenge is stopping being useful for long enough to simply be in the room they made so welcoming for everyone else.
