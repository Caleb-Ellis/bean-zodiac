People born in the Year of the Sweet Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most reliably welcoming souls — warm, adaptable, and constitutionally inclined to make whoever is nearby feel at ease. The sweetness in their character makes their warmth land as delight rather than as effort. Their complication is a deeply habitual usefulness that sometimes prevents them from simply enjoying the room.
