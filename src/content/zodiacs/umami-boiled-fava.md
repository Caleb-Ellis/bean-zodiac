Boiled Beans born in the Year of the Umami Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most vigilant — they never stop watching what matters.

Fava Beans understand that what matters requires effort to access and keep; Umami Beans provide slow, careful attention that notices by accumulation; Boiled Beans make it sustained and consistent.

The Boiled Umami Fava Bean's challenge is rest. Sustained vigilance without relief distorts the picture, and they must stand down occasionally to stay accurate.
