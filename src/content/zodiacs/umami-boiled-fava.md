Dashi Fava Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most vigilant — they never stop watching what matters.

Fava Beans understand that what matters requires effort to access and keep; Umami Beans provide slow, careful attention that notices by accumulation; Boiled Beans make it sustained and consistent.

The Dashi Fava Bean's challenge is rest. Sustained vigilance without relief distorts the picture, and they must stand down occasionally to stay accurate.
