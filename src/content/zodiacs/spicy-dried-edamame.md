Dried Beans born in the Year of the Spicy Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most caustic — efficient, direct, and not always aware of what the efficiency costs.

Edamame Beans reach conclusions while others are still framing the question; Spicy Beans make impact total and immediate; Dried Beans strip away anything that might have softened the landing.

The Dried Spicy Edamame's challenge is the relationship damaged by accuracy. Being right is not the same as being useful. These Beans must decide, before they speak, whether the precision serves the situation or only themselves.
