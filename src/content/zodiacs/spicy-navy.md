People born in the Year of the Spicy Navy Bean are the reliable, foundational people who have, in at least one surprising season, decided to be undeniable. They have the Navy Bean's deep steadiness and community-building instinct alongside a heat that occasionally pushes them out of their natural preference for the background and into a room that needs to know they exist. They do not seek the spotlight. They are capable of it, more than most people expect, and when they choose to use it the effect is memorable in a way that plain white bean soup, excellent as it is, simply is not.
