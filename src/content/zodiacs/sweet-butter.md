---
bean: butter
flavour: sweet
dish: Butter bean mash with honey-roasted carrots on the side. Everything warm, everything fine.
fortune: Abundance is not suspicious. Take the second helping, and do not spend this soft year wondering if you have done enough to deserve it.
---

People born in the Year of the Sweet Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most genuinely nourishing souls — warm and giving in the way that a really good meal is warm and giving, without agenda or caveat. They are constitutionally suited to making people feel cared for, and the sweetness in their character means their generosity lands as delight rather than obligation. Their complication, if they have one, is a tendency to keep giving even when the nourishment could reasonably be allowed to flow back toward them. They are learning, slowly and somewhat reluctantly, to take the second helping.
