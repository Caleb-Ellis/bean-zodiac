People born in the Year of the Sweet Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most calming — easygoing, unhurried, and constitutionally suited to the kind of stillness that other people find surprisingly difficult.

The Sweet Flavour amplifies their natural ease into something that feels like a gift to the room: a still point around which everyone else quietly recalibrates.

The Sweet Butter Bean's challenge is noticing when rest has become inertia — the two feel identical from the inside.
