People born in the Year of the Sweet Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most genuinely restful souls — easygoing, unhurried, and constitutionally suited to the kind of stillness that other people find surprisingly difficult. The Sweet Flavour amplifies their natural ease into something that feels like a gift to the room: a still point around which everyone else quietly recalibrates. Their complication is a tendency to let rest become inertia without noticing the difference.
