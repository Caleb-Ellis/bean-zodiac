People born in the Year of the Spicy Adzuki are the ones responsible for the legendary party — not because everything went smoothly, but because something unexpected happened and they rolled with it so magnificently that it became the whole point. They carry the Adzuki's deep festive instinct alongside a heat and boldness that means their good times have genuine weight to them. They are not interested in the polite gathering where nothing goes wrong. They want the night that becomes a story, and they have an uncanny talent for finding it.
