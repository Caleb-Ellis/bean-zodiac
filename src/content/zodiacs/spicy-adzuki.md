People born in the Year of the Spicy Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most electrifying — responsible for the legendary gathering, not because everything went smoothly, but because something unexpected happened and they met it with such presence that it became the whole point.

They carry the Adzuki's deep festive instinct alongside a heat and boldness that means their good times have genuine stakes. They are not interested in the polite event where nothing goes wrong.

The Spicy Adzuki Bean's challenge is appreciating the quiet, ordinary celebration — the one where nothing goes wrong and nothing becomes a story.
