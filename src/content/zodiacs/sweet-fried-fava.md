Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fried Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most daring — they take the leap with a warmth and ease that makes it look like something anyone might do, which is how they end up doing things that most people never attempt.

Fava Bean moves toward the difficult as a matter of instinct; Sweet strips out the calculation and the conditions that slow others down — the dare is accepted from warmth rather than recklessness; Fried means the decision and the action arrive as one, nothing cooling between them. These Beans don't gather their courage. They simply find themselves already going.

The Sweet Fried Fava Bean's challenge is the aftermath. Daring that lands well is celebrated; daring that doesn't still needs to be accounted for. These Beans must develop as much ease with the reckoning as with the leap.
