Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most attentive — they are fully present in ways most people manage only occasionally, and the people they attend to can feel the difference.

Edamame brings efficiency to bearing — no distractions, no split attention, the task in front of them is the task; Umami gives this attentiveness its warmth and depth, so what these Beans offer is not merely focus but genuine presence; Roasted means it radiates outward — the person being attended to feels, clearly, that they are the whole room.

The Umami Roasted Edamame's challenge is that attentiveness can be misread as availability. These Beans must distinguish between being fully present and being endlessly accessible. Not everyone who enters the room deserves the whole warmth.
