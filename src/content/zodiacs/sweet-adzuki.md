People born in the Year of the Sweet Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most naturally auspicious souls — warm, joyful, and constitutionally suited to celebration in the way that red bean paste is suited to mochi. They have the Adzuki's gift for festivity in its most uncomplicated form, aligned with a sweetness that makes their luck feel effortless and their joy feel warranted. Their one challenge is a modesty that occasionally causes them to miss their own most auspicious moments.
