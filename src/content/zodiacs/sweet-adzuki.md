People born in the Year of the Sweet Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most joyful — warm, celebratory, and constitutionally suited to festivity in the way that red bean paste is suited to mochi. They have the Adzuki's gift for auspiciousness in its most uncomplicated form, aligned with a sweetness that makes their luck feel effortless and their joy feel warranted.

The Sweet Adzuki Bean's challenge is recognising their own most auspicious moments without someone else pointing them out first.
