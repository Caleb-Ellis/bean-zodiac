Beans born in the Boiled Season of the Bitter Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most dogmatic — they apply a framework fully, conviction embedded so deeply it becomes invisible.

Navy Bean believes in systems it has tested; Bitter Beans strip away what hasn't earned merit; Boiled Beans steep conviction so slowly it becomes invisible to them.

The Boiled Bitter Navy Bean's challenge is the system outlived by its evidence. A principle with no memory of its origin is inherited, not principled. They must ask whether the structure still serves what it was built for.
