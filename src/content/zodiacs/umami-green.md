People born in the Year of the Umami Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most layered — fresh and energetic by nature, but carrying a growing Umami richness they didn't necessarily cultivate on purpose.

They are more interesting than they initially present as. The combination of genuine freshness and genuine depth is a rare thing, and they are only beginning to understand what they have.

The Umami Green Bean's challenge is learning to move at the speed of depth rather than the speed of freshness — the two don't always run at the same pace.
