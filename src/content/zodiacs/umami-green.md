People born in the Year of the Umami Green Bean are the quick-moving people who contain, somewhat to their own surprise, genuine depth. They have the Green Bean's natural speed and vivid presence alongside a Umami richness that developed without announcement — a depth they did not necessarily cultivate on purpose and have not quite finished discovering. They are more complex than they present as. They have more to say than they have said. The miso was always part of the butter; they just did not know it yet. This year, or this life, is when they find out.
