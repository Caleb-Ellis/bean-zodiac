Dried Beans born in the Year of the Bitter Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most cryptic — the markings are there, the meaning is not offered freely.

Pinto Beans wear their interior openly; Bitter Beans insist that meaning must be earned; Dried Beans strip away the readily given, leaving only what withstands scrutiny.

The Dried Bitter Pinto Bean's challenge is being understood at all. A story told in code reaches no one. These Beans must occasionally offer the unguarded version — not the edited one.
