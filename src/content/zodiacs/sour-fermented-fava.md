Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most contrarian — they don't hold the minority position to be difficult; they hold it because they have looked at the majority position from every angle and found somewhere it doesn't hold.

Fava Bean is instinctively suspicious of anything that requires no effort; Sour has no patience for arguments that survive only because no one has pressed them; Fermented means the contrarian position has been worked on privately until it is tight enough to actually sustain pressure. These Beans are not contrarian for effect. They simply keep finding the place where the consensus is soft.

The Sour Fermented Fava Bean's challenge is that contrarianism can become a default rather than a conclusion. If the instinct is always to push back, it stops being a response and becomes a posture. These Beans must occasionally find themselves agreeing.
