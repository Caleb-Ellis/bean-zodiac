Chili-Dried Butter Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most volatile — the calm is present, until suddenly it isn't.

Butter Beans hold warmth and ease as their natural state; Spicy Beans introduce heat that demands full attention; Dried Beans concentrate the reaction, making any shift from rest to intensity immediate and total.

The Chili-Dried Butter Bean's challenge is the surprise of their own temperature. These Beans rarely see the transition coming. They must learn to sense what's building in time to direct it — rather than discovering it has arrived.
