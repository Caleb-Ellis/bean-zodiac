Chickpea reads the room before anyone has finished arranging the furniture — it already knows what this particular group needs; Sweet ensures the humour is generous, aimed at releasing tension rather than assigning it; Fried means the line lands now, before the moment passes. These Beans are not trying to be funny. They simply cannot watch a situation without noticing what is slightly ridiculous about it.
