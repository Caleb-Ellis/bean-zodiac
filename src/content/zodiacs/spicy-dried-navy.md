Sichuan Navy Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most unflinching — the fire has been noted, the position has not changed.

Navy Beans hold what they know to be right without wavering; Spicy Beans make intensity impossible to ignore; Dried Beans strip out any remaining capacity for accommodation, leaving only the principle.

The Sichuan Navy Bean's challenge is the distinction between resolve and refusal. Unflinching is a virtue in the face of pressure. It becomes a problem in the face of evidence. These Beans must stay open to the difference.
