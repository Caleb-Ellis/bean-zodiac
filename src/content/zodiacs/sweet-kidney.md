People born in the Year of the Sweet Kidney Bean are the strong, vital people who have, after considerable effort, arrived somewhere genuinely good — and are slowly, cautiously, allowing themselves to enjoy it. They have the Kidney Bean's deep earthiness and physical vitality alongside a sweetness that means their strength has somewhere wonderful to land rather than just somewhere worthy. They have been fighting for things worth having. They are the people who finally got to eat the mochi. Their ongoing project is learning to accept that the battle is over and that they are allowed to simply be at the celebration.
