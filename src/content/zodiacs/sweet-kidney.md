People born in the Year of the Sweet Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most wholeheartedly loving souls — strong, vital, and in a Sweet Year, able to direct all that passion somewhere that simply receives it with equal warmth. The sweetness in their character means their intensity lands as devotion rather than as demand. Their ongoing work is learning that the threat is not ongoing, and that they are allowed to stop protecting and simply enjoy what they love.
