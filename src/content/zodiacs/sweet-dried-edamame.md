Dried Beans born in the Year of the Sweet Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most uncomplicated — they have removed everything that was not necessary, and what is left is simply good.

Edamame Beans find the efficient path; Sweet Beans provide warmth without requiring effort; Dried Beans strip everything to its most essential form.

The Dried Sweet Edamame's challenge is the simplicity that becomes a ceiling. Not everything resolves into something clean — and a Bean that refuses complexity will sometimes refuse growth. These Beans must let the harder thing in occasionally.
