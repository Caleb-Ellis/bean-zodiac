Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Sour Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most wispy — their care passes through rooms without settling into any of them.

Mung Beans tend without demanding; Sour Beans give the care an unsentimental edge; Smoked Beans let both qualities drift rather than land.

The Smoked Sour Mung Bean's challenge is grounding. Care that doesn't stay can't repair what it passes through. These Beans must find a place to put their attention down and leave it there.
