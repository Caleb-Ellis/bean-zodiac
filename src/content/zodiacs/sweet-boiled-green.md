Candied Green Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most playful — lightness that sustains itself over time.

Green Beans move and explore freely; Sweet Beans offer this without score-keeping; Boiled Beans make it compound, running long and sustainably.

The Candied Green Bean's challenge is being taken seriously. They must occasionally hold still long enough for others to notice the substance beneath the motion.
