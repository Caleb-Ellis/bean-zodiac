Boiled Beans born in the Year of the Sweet Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most compassionate — they have the patience to sit with difficulty and the warmth to make it bearable.

Kidney Beans protect those who are hurting; Sweet Beans open that into generosity without condition; Boiled Beans make it long and steady, not brief.

The Boiled Sweet Kidney Bean's challenge is receiving as well as giving. They must allow the Beans they sustain to sustain them in return.
