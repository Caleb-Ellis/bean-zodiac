People born in the Year of the Bitter Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most curious — sociable and warm, but slowly learning the rewards of depth over breadth. They have the Chickpea's extraordinary adaptability, but the Bitter Flavour keeps asking them to stay somewhere long enough to find out what's actually there. They are excellent at entering any room. They are becoming very good at choosing which rooms to stay in.

The Bitter Chickpea's challenge is staying long enough to find out what a room actually has, rather than moving on to the next interesting one.
