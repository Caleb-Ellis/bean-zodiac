People born in the Year of the Sweet Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most sustaining — people who carry genuine depth and do foundational, invisible work that makes everything around them better, and who are sometimes surprised when the sweetness of life finds them.

The darkness under the brownie is what makes it extraordinary rather than merely nice.

The Sweet Black Bean's challenge is receiving good fortune without looking for the catch.
