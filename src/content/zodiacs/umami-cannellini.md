People born in the Year of the Umami Cannellini are quietly, steadily exceptional — the kind of people who make everything they touch slightly better, without flash or announcement, through patience and accumulated refinement. They have the Cannellini's elegance alongside a depth that builds over time in the way of a good long braise: invisible while it's happening, undeniable when it's done. Their great challenge is release. They sit on things — ideas, work, feelings — long after the right moment, refining past the point of necessity. What they are holding is already more than enough.
