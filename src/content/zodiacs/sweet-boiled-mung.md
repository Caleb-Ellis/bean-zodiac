Beans born in the Boiled Season of the Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most nurturing — they tend conditions quietly, ensuring others have what they need before it's named.

Mung Beans build the environment without notice; Sweet Beans make it generous and genuinely felt; Boiled Beans hold it in sustained attention, not bursts.

The Boiled Sweet Mung Bean's challenge is reciprocity. They must allow others to take care of them, not become invisible through endless giving.
