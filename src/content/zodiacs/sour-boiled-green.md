Brined Green Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most restless — always adjusting between the need to move and the need to stay.

Green Beans cannot stop generating new directions; Sour Beans cut away what doesn't hold up; Boiled Beans require staying somewhere long enough.

The Brined Green Bean's challenge is accepting the current state. They must learn to allow something to be enough for now.
