Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Roasted Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most prudent — they have a gift for anticipating what could go wrong that others experience as pessimism, and that those paying closer attention recognise as foresight.

Navy Bean is principled and dependable, built on the understanding that systems work until they fail; Bitter brings the analytical intelligence to identify precisely where the failure might come; Roasted holds the whole thing at a warmth that keeps the analysis from curdling into paralysis. These Beans do not expect disaster — they prepare for it, and are quietly ready when it arrives.

The Bitter Roasted Navy Bean's challenge is the contingency that becomes the plan. Prudence that never becomes commitment is hesitation with better vocabulary. These Beans must let the preparation become an action.
