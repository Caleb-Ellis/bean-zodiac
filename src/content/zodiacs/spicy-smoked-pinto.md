Chipotle Pinto Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most erratic — the expressiveness is vivid, the temperature is high, and the direction changes before it can be followed.

Pinto Beans carry their complexity openly and colour everything around them; Spicy Beans make each shift feel immediate and total; Smoked Beans let the whole pattern run from a source that cannot be reliably read.

The Chipotle Pinto Bean's challenge is pattern. Erratic at high temperature eventually exhausts the room. These Beans must find one thread and follow it far enough to show where it leads.
