dish: Mung bean sprout salad with rice vinegar and sesame. Fresh, sharp, more than just mild.
fortune: Ask the harder question — you can handle the answer, and the person you are asking it of can too. Kindness and clarity are not opposites.

People born in the Year of the Sour Mung Bean are the gentle truth-tellers — the healers who insist on also knowing what caused the wound. They have the Mung Bean's deep restorative instincts and cooling grace alongside a Sour honesty that refuses to let kindness become avoidance. They are not harsh people; they are clear ones, which is different and considerably more useful. They will sit with you through the difficult thing and also, gently and specifically, name what the difficult thing actually is. The vinegar does not ruin the sprouts. It makes them a dish.
