Crystallised Butter Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most serene — the ease has been purified of the softness, leaving something still and enduring.

Butter Beans lower the temperature wherever they are; Sweet Beans extend comfort without effort; Dried Beans strip away the indulgence, distilling what remains into something durable.

The Crystallised Butter Bean's challenge is the peace that has become detachment. There is a version of serenity that is wisdom, and a version that is simply being very far away. These Beans must occasionally close the distance.
