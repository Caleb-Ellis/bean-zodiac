People born in the Year of the Umami Chickpea are the connectors who have arrived at depth. They have the Chickpea's extraordinary social gifts and warmth, now deepened by an Umami richness that rewards existing relationships over new ones. They are the people around whom other people naturally gather — not because they are performing, but because being near them is genuinely sustaining. Their natural instinct is to widen the circle. Their great discovery is that deepening it is more interesting. The people already in their orbit are the main event, and they are slowly, happily, coming to understand this.
