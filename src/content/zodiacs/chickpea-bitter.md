tagline: You can be anything. This year, try being one thing.

The chickpea's great skill is fitting everywhere — hummus, falafel, curry, salad, roasted with whatever spice you have to hand — and that skill is genuinely impressive. Bitter, however, is not impressed by versatility. Bitter is the flavour that says: pick a lane and stay in it long enough to find out what's actually there. The best hummus takes time. You can't make it well if you keep stopping to become something else. This year, one commitment made seriously will be worth more than five kept loosely.

**Fortune for this year:** The best version of you isn't the most versions of you. Blend.
