Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Boiled Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most agreeable — they meet people where those people are, without making a performance of the effort, and something about the ease of this makes the people around them feel unexpectedly settled.

Chickpea makes itself at home in any company without losing what it is, holding its shape or dissolving into something new; Sweet extends this as genuine generosity, warm and open; Boiled adds the patience to remain present and reliable over a long stretch, without flagging. Being with these Beans is easy in a way that is, once experienced, difficult to stop seeking out.

The Sweet Boiled Chickpea's challenge is their own preferences. Agreeable Beans are so good at accommodation that they sometimes forget to notice what they themselves want. These Beans must occasionally hold out for the thing that is also good for them.
