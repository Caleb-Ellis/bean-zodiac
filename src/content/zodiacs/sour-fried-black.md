Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fried Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most direct — what they see, they say; what they mean, they name; the gap between thought and expression is as small as it gets in any Bean.

Black Bean has already done the watching; Sour cuts immediately to what is precise and true, stripping everything down to its necessary parts; Fried delivers it at heat, without waiting to see if a softer moment is coming. These Beans are not blunt for the pleasure of it — they simply have no patience for the parts of a conversation that don't carry information.

The Sour Fried Black Bean's challenge is the landing. Directness without warmth can read as dismissal. These Beans must ensure that the absence of ornament is not mistaken for the absence of care.
