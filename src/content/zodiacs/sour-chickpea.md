People born in the Year of the Sour Chickpea are the adaptable, warm-natured souls who are in the process of discovering which version of their adaptability is the genuine one. They have the Chickpea's remarkable social gifts and ease in any situation, but the Sour Flavour in them acts like the squeeze of lemon over the roasted bowl — sharpening and defining, making everything more specifically itself. They are people who know how to fit in, and who are slowly figuring out that knowing who they actually are is the more interesting project.
