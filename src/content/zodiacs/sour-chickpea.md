People born in the Year of the Sour Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most warmly honest companions — adaptable and socially gifted, but with a Sour clarity that keeps asking which part of that adaptability is genuinely theirs. They know how to fit in anywhere, and they are doing the interesting work of figuring out where they actually belong. Both things are true simultaneously, and that is fine.
