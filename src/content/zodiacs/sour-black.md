People born in the Year of the Sour Black Bean carry depth as a default state and are only slowly learning that legibility is not the enemy of complexity. They have the Black Bean's natural mystery and preference for keeping their own counsel, but the Sour Flavour gives them a crisp edge — a willingness, eventually, to let the private opinion become the public one. They are not natural oversharers. But they are people who, when they finally do say the thing they've been thinking, tend to change the room. The lime was always part of the soup.
