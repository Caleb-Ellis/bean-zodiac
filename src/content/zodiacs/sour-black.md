People born in the Year of the Sour Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most honest — people who carry genuine depth and are learning that clarity does not threaten complexity. They have the Black Bean's preference for keeping their own counsel, but the Sour Flavour gives them a crisp edge — a willingness, when it matters, to let the private opinion become the public one. When they do say the thing they have been thinking, they tend to change the room.

The Sour Black Bean's challenge is saying the private opinion before they have refined it into something too sharp to survive contact with the room.
