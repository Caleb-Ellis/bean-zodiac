Dried Beans born in the Year of the Bitter Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most ascetic — they have removed every excess and arrived at something so refined it barely takes up space.

Cannellini Beans curate ruthlessly; Bitter Beans insist everything earn its presence; Dried Beans complete the reduction, leaving only what cannot be argued away.

The Dried Bitter Cannellini Bean's challenge is the life unlived by omission. Perfection achieved through removal is still removal. These Beans must occasionally allow something imperfect to remain.
