Black Beans keep their depth private; Umami Beans reward those who stay long enough to understand; Dried Beans concentrate the depth until it is all that is left.

The Dried Umami Black Bean's challenge is the profundity that never surfaces. Insight is only useful when it reaches someone. These Beans must occasionally speak what they know, rather than waiting to be asked.
