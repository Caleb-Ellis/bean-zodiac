Aged Black Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most profound — they have been attending to the deep currents for so long that surface events rarely concern them.

Black Beans keep their depth private; Umami Beans reward those who stay long enough to understand; Dried Beans concentrate the depth until it is all that is left.

The Aged Black Bean's challenge is the profundity that never surfaces. Insight is only useful when it reaches someone. These Beans must occasionally speak what they know, rather than waiting to be asked.
