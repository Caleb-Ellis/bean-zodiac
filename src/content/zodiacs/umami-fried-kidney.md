Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fried Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most moralistic — they have developed strong convictions about how things should go, and they are not prepared to stay quiet while they go another way.

Umami Fried Kidney Beans act from principle. Kidney Bean's protective instinct extends naturally to what it believes is right; Umami provides the depth and the history — this is not a new position, it has been earned through sustained engagement; Fried means it arrives without delay, at conviction temperature, in a form the listener cannot ignore. These Beans have reasons for what they believe, and the reasons are good. The issue, occasionally, is the delivery.

The Umami Fried Kidney Bean's challenge is the distance between conviction and persuasion. A strongly held principle, delivered at heat, closes as many doors as it opens. These Beans must find ways to bring others toward what they believe — not just to announce it.
