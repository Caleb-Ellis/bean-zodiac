People born in the Year of the Sour Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most original — expressive and vivid, but with a Sour clarity steadily distinguishing their own marks from the marks that other people's stories have left on them. The editing is not a loss. What remains is more beautiful than the complicated whole — specific rather than just vivid, and entirely theirs.

The Sour Pinto Bean's challenge is letting the editing stop — what remains when the unnecessary is gone doesn't need to be refined further.
