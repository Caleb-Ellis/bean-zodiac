They have the Mung Bean's cooling, quietly medicinal nature alongside a Bitter patience that makes their care deeply thorough rather than merely kind. They do not rush people through difficult things. They sit with them in the difficult thing until the thing actually resolves, which often takes longer than everyone expected and is therefore more complete. They work at low temperature, quietly, and almost always without credit. The results are the kind that last.
