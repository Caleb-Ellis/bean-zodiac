Beans born in the Season of the Sour Boiled Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most sarcastic — they have been holding that line for a while, and they waited until the timing was exactly right.

Pinto Bean expresses what it sees, its markings a running commentary on an interior that never fully quiets; Sour turns this toward clarity and sharpness, precision that can leave a mark; Boiled steeps the whole arrangement in patience until the observation is fully formed and the phrasing is exactly what it needs to be. The delivery is impeccable because it was never rushed.

The Sour Boiled Pinto Bean's challenge is altitude. Sarcasm well-aimed is clarifying; sustained, it is corrosive. These Beans must remember that the sharpest thing is sometimes sincerity.
