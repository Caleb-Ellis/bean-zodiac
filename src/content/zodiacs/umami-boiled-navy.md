Dashi Navy Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most pedantic — the difference between right and nearly right is not a small difference to them.

Navy Beans understand that systems work for a reason; Umami Beans make the distinction between correct and incorrect feel significant; Boiled Beans steep everything in patience until detail becomes principle.

The Dashi Navy Bean's challenge is perspective. They must occasionally step back and ask whether the whole deserves the precision devoted to its parts.
