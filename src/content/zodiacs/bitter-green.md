People born in the Year of the Bitter Green Bean are the fastest people in the Bean Zodiac who are learning, with considerable effort, to appreciate slowness. They have the Green Bean's natural momentum and vivid presence — immediate, fresh, always in motion — and the Bitter flavour in their character keeps asking them to acquire a taste for things that do not arrive immediately. They are not bad at patience; they are people for whom patience requires actual work. This gives them an unusual quality: they have all the freshness of someone who moves fast and all the depth of someone who has stayed.
