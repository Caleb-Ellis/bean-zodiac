People born in the Year of the Bitter Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most enthusiastic — bright and in constant motion, with a Bitter edge that keeps asking them to stop long enough to appreciate what takes more than a first pass.

They have the Green Bean's natural momentum and enthusiasm, sharpened by a discernment that is slowly developing a taste for complexity. They do not lack depth — they are simply more used to moving quickly than staying still.

The Bitter Green Bean's challenge is giving the pause the same energy they give the motion — without treating it as the thing standing between them and the next thing.
