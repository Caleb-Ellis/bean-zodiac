People born in the Year of the Spicy Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most compassionate — nurturers who can also, when someone needs to hear it, be entirely direct. They are the people you go to when you need to feel better and also know what to do. Their gift is holding both things at once: the warmth that makes hard truths land gently, and the directness that makes the gentleness actually useful.

The Spicy Mung Bean's challenge is recognising when someone needs only the warmth — and not the directness that usually comes with it.
