People born in the Year of the Spicy Mung Bean are the healers who can also, when necessary, be direct. They have the Mung Bean's cooling, restorative gentleness alongside a Spicy warmth that means their care has real force behind it — they are not whispering when someone needs to actually hear them. They are the people you go to when you need to feel better and also know what to do. Their gift is holding both things at once: the warmth that makes hard truths land gently, and the clarity that makes gentle care actually work. They do not have to choose between soft and honest.
