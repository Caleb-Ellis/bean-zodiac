People born in the Year of the Bitter Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most discerning — generous and genuinely festive, but unwilling to waste their joy on something that hasn't earned it. They carry the Adzuki's deep instinct for auspicious occasions, sharpened by a Bitter discernment that makes their warmth specific rather than general. Their celebrations are not performances — they are verdicts. When these people decide something is worth celebrating, it genuinely is.

The Bitter Adzuki Bean's challenge is knowing when the occasion is good enough — and not waiting for the perfect moment until the moment has passed.
