People born in the Year of the Bitter Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's wittiest — easygoing by nature, with a Bitter edge that keeps one eyebrow perpetually raised. They are genuinely calm; they are also genuinely amused. They have a gift for noticing the absurdity in a situation and saying so in precisely the fewest words required. People in their orbit find them deeply restful and occasionally startling.

The Bitter Butter Bean's challenge is remembering that not everyone finds the absurdity as immediately visible as they do — or as interesting.
