People born in the Year of the Bitter Butter Bean are among the warmest and most generous in the zodiac — but they are not soft in the way that can be taken advantage of. The bitterness in their character is not coldness; it is discernment. It cuts through their natural richness and makes the whole person more interesting than warmth alone would be. They know who deserves their softness and who has simply been coasting on it, and over time they become very good at directing their considerable generosity toward people who are genuinely worth it.
