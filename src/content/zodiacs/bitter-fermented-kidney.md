Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most intense — they carry more beneath the surface than most Beans will ever show above it.

Bitter Fermented Kidney Beans work at a level others cannot see. Kidney Bean's passion runs deep and its protective instinct runs deeper; Bitter refuses to surface anything until it has been fully examined; Fermented takes all of this inward and processes it without fire, on its own schedule, until what remains is something precise and undeniably real. These Beans are rarely legible at first contact. They are very legible, eventually, to those who wait.

The Bitter Fermented Kidney Bean's challenge is not becoming so compressed that nothing can escape. Intensity is not the same as containment. These Beans must find a way to let what they carry be felt — not just eventually known.
