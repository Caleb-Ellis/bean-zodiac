Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Bitter Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most reticent — they see the situation immediately, and they say very little about it.

Edamame Beans cut through noise and reach the conclusion first; Bitter Beans refuse to share what hasn't been earned; Smoked Beans let the understanding hang in the air without declaration.

The Smoked Bitter Edamame's challenge is transmission. Conclusions kept entirely to oneself become indistinguishable from having had none. These Beans must share the finding before it expires.
