Zested Pinto Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most astringent — the expressiveness remains, but the sentimentality has been cut away entirely.

Pinto Beans wear their interior openly; Sour Beans clarify what is real from what is performance; Dried Beans hold the result in concentrated form, without decoration.

The Zested Pinto Bean's challenge is the audience lost to clarity. Art that has stripped away all softness is not always art anyone wants to sit with. These Beans must remember that feeling, even imprecise feeling, is also real.
