Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Boiled Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most worrisome — they feel the urgency of every potential problem and hold it steadily in place, unable to put it down.

Mung Bean tends the condition and monitors for decline; Spicy makes every risk feel immediate and pressing; Boiled refuses to rush to resolution, holding the whole thing in slow suspension. The worry is not catastrophising — it is care without a clear object, looking for somewhere to land. These Beans feel responsible for things they cannot fix and track risks that others do not think to consider.

The Spicy Boiled Mung Bean's challenge is release. Not every contingency can be prepared for, and not every preparation helps. These Beans must occasionally set the worry down without resolving it.
