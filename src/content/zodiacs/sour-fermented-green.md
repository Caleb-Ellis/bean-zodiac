Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most quirky — they have taken Green Bean's fresh energy into a fermented dark, cut it with Sour's refusal to normalise, and emerged with something that cannot be easily categorised.

Green Bean starts with an openness to any direction; Fermented has been working those directions privately, producing outcomes no one predicted; Sour clears away the polite consensus that might otherwise smooth the result into something more acceptable. These Beans do not arrive at the strange on purpose — the strange is just what happened when no one was looking.

The Sour Fermented Green Bean's challenge is being legible to others. The quirky path can be invisible to those who haven't walked it. These Beans must occasionally describe the route, not only the destination.
