Chili-Dried Black Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most smoldering — nothing visible on the surface, but the heat inside has never gone out.

Black Beans hold everything below the surface; Spicy Beans make intensity the whole condition; Dried Beans concentrate it and seal it in, with no outlet.

The Chili-Dried Black Bean's challenge is combustion without cause. Intensity held indefinitely without release does not resolve — it accumulates. These Beans must find a way to let some of it show before it takes an unintended form.
