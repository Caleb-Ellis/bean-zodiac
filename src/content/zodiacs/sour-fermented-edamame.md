Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most skeptical — not cynical, but constitutionally resistant to accepting things at the level they are offered. They need to know what is underneath.

Edamame doesn't slow down for surface performance; Sour has no patience for premises that don't hold; Fermented means the investigation runs long and in private, turning the question in the dark until the genuine shape of it emerges. These Beans have caught too many things that looked different under closer inspection. Their skepticism is earned.

The Sour Fermented Edamame's challenge is knowing when to stop. Skepticism that never arrives at conviction is another form of avoidance. These Beans must eventually decide what they believe.
