Desiccated Black Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most laconic — they say exactly one thing, and it is the right one.

Black Beans give nothing before it's time; Bitter Beans strip away what doesn't earn its place; Dried Beans reduce until only what is essential remains.

The Desiccated Black Bean's challenge is the cost of silence. A Bean that never speaks wastes its own accuracy. These Beans must trust that the one thing worth saying is worth saying aloud.
