Candied Adzuki Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most joyful — joy as constitutional orientation, not occasion.

Adzuki Beans mark what deserves marking; Sweet Beans offer it without condition; Boiled Beans carry the transformation all the way through.

The Candied Adzuki Bean's challenge is knowing that joy requires tending, not just feeling it. They must ensure their joy is meeting life, not insulating from it.
