Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fried Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most overachieving — whatever the task, they are already planning how to do twice as much, and somehow, frequently, managing it.

Green Bean arrives ready and immediately looking for the next thing; Umami means there is actual substance behind the ambition — these are not gestures at high performance but genuine depth; Fried provides the temperature at which all of it accelerates, removing whatever interval might otherwise exist between deciding and doing. These Beans do not coast. The idea of coasting has not occurred to them.

The Umami Fried Green Bean's challenge is the expectation they set. The Beans around them acclimate to the output and begin to depend on it. These Beans must allow themselves to be ordinary — occasionally, briefly, without apology.
