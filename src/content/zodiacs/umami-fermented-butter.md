Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fermented Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most reflective — they do not leave an experience behind without first going back into it, finding what was there, and staying until they understand what it was trying to say.

Butter Bean is in no hurry to move on; Umami does not reward speed — the depth it offers comes only to those who stay present long enough to receive it; Fermented takes the whole operation internal, working in conditions no one else can observe, producing something richer than what the original moment seemed to contain. These Beans are students of their own experience — not in a self-absorbed way, but in the way of someone who has noticed that things repay attention, and who has made a practice of paying it.

The Umami Fermented Butter Bean's challenge is forward momentum. Reflection is nourishment — it is also, occasionally, a way of not having to decide what comes next. These Beans must know the difference between staying with something because it still has something to give, and staying because leaving requires them to choose.
