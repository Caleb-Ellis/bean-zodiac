Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most critical — not harsh, but constitutionally unable to look at something without knowing exactly how it could be better.

Sour Fermented Cannellini Beans process in private. Cannellini Bean curates ruthlessly — its instinct is to refine and elevate; Sour cuts through sentiment and softens nothing; Fermented takes the discernment inward and works on it until what emerges is a conclusion, not an opinion. These Beans have usually known for some time what they are about to tell you — they have simply been waiting until they were certain.

The Sour Fermented Cannellini Bean's challenge is delivery. A conclusion arrived at through long fermentation does not always land gently. These Beans must remember that precision without warmth is just sharpness — and the Cannellini Bean has always known how to add the olive oil.
