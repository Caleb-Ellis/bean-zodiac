People born in the Year of the Bitter Fava Bean carry something genuinely old in them — a patience and groundedness that reads less like a personality trait and more like inherited knowledge. They have the Fava Bean's historical weight and quiet courage, deepened by a Bitter flavour that asks for time and rewards those who give it. They are not in a hurry, and they are not apologetic about this. They understand, with a certainty that is difficult to explain and impossible to fake, that some things take as long as they take. The meal that results is the one that has been eaten since antiquity for good reason.
