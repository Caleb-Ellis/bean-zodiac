Dried Beans born in the Year of the Sweet Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most storied — the marks are faded but the flavour they left is still in everything.

Pinto Beans carry their histories openly; Sweet Beans extend warmth forward without conditions; Dried Beans hold the whole of it, concentrated in something small and lasting.

The Dried Sweet Pinto Bean's challenge is living in the story rather than the present. A history rich enough can become a place to live. These Beans must let today be part of the record — not only what came before.
