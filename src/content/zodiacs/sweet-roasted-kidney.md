Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most overprotective — the warmth is real, the care is genuine, and the difficulty is that both have a radius that is very hard to leave.

Sweet Roasted Kidney Beans create an atmosphere. Kidney Bean colours everything it touches and does not release its hold easily; Sweet makes this generous and inviting; Roasted radiates all of it outward in a warmth that fills the whole space. What you receive inside that warmth is real — nourishment, safety, full welcome. What becomes difficult is the awareness, creeping in eventually, that the warmth has a shape, and the shape is theirs, and they are watching to make sure you stay inside it.

The Sweet Roasted Kidney Bean's challenge is understanding that love expressed as enclosure is still enclosure. These Beans must find ways to care that allow the people they love to move freely — and to trust that freedom does not mean departure.
