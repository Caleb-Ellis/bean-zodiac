Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fermented Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most obsessive — they have found the thing, and they are not done with it, and they are not asking permission to continue.

Spicy Fermented Kidney Beans work without an audience. Kidney Bean runs at depth; Spicy demands full attention, and then again, and then again after that; Fermented seals all of this away from outside input and lets it transform on its own terms, according to its own logic, until what started as intensity has become something closer to a fixed star. These Beans do not move between interests. They move further into one.

The Spicy Fermented Kidney Bean's challenge is not mistaking focus for truth. What an obsession reveals about the world is real; what it reveals about the self is also real — and sometimes that second revelation is the one worth examining. These Beans must occasionally surface and check their bearings.
