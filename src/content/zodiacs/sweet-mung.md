People born in the Year of the Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most genuinely nurturing souls — warm, gentle, and constitutionally inclined toward making things better for people without requiring anything in return. Their gift is making nourishment feel safe and easy. Their complication is a slight difficulty accepting that they are, sometimes, the person who needs to be looked after. They are allowed to be.
