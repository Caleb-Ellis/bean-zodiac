People born in the Year of the Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most genuinely restorative souls — warm, undemanding, and constitutionally inclined toward making things better without requiring anything in return. They have the Mung Bean's quiet healing gifts in their sweetest expression: the bowl of warm soup offered without conditions, the care that does not come with a list of requirements. Their gift is making ease feel safe. Their complication, if they have one, is a slight difficulty accepting that they themselves are sometimes the person who needs the soup. They are allowed to be.
