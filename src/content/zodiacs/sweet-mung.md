People born in the Year of the Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most generous — warm, gentle, and constitutionally inclined toward making things better for people without requiring anything in return. Their gift is making nourishment feel safe and easy.

The Sweet Mung Bean's challenge is accepting care as readily as they offer it — being looked after is something they are allowed.
