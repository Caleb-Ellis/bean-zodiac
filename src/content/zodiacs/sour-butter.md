People born in the Year of the Sour Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most trustworthy — warm and easygoing, but willing to clearly tell you what the situation actually is. Their honesty is a form of their warmth, not its opposite. They are the people whose difficult conversations feel like care rather than criticism.

The Sour Butter Bean's challenge is trusting that the warmth survives the honest thing they just said.
