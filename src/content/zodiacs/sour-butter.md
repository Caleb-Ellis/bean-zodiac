People born in the Year of the Sour Butter Bean are the warm truth-tellers — the ones who will sit with you through something hard and also, gently but clearly, tell you what it actually is. They have the Butter Bean's deep warmth and comfort-giving nature, but the Sour flavour gives them an honest edge that prevents their kindness from curdling into avoidance. They are the people whose difficult conversations feel like care rather than criticism. They know that a little acid in the braise does not ruin it. It makes it taste like what it actually is.
