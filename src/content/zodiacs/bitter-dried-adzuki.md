Desiccated Adzuki Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most selective — they know exactly what deserves celebration, and they will not spend it on anything less.

Adzuki Beans hold their festivity for the genuinely auspicious; Bitter Beans strip away what hasn't earned it; Dried Beans ensure that what remains is concentrated, not diluted.

The Desiccated Adzuki Bean's challenge is the celebration withheld. Joy that never finds its occasion is not discernment — it is avoidance. These Beans must allow that some moments are worthy even before they have been verified.
