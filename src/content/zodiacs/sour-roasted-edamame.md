Beans born in the Season of the Sour Roasted Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most forthright — they volunteer the truth before they are asked for it, and they do this from warmth, not compulsion.

Edamame doesn't waste a turn at table; Sour means whatever they offer is honest, with no flattering vagueness around it; Roasted gives the honesty a heat that makes it feel like a gift rather than a verdict. These Beans tell you the thing that would have taken three months to work out on your own, and somehow it doesn't sting.

The Sour Roasted Edamame's challenge is that forthrightness offered too readily can foreclose someone else's discovery. These Beans must occasionally let a person find out for themselves.
