People born in the Year of the Umami Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most patient developers of depth. They are not interested in announcing progress or beginning new things for the pleasure of beginnings. They tend one thing at a time, quietly and with great commitment, and they trust the low heat. Two kinds of invisible richness — the Black Bean's quiet complexity and the Umami's slow accumulation — combine in these people to produce something that is genuinely nourishing and genuinely rare. Almost nobody knows it yet. That is exactly how they prefer it.
