Aged Chickpeas are the Bean Zodiac's most substantive — they move widely but carry real weight, and whatever room they enter is the heavier for it.

Chickpeas find common ground across any divide; Umami Beans bring depth that accumulates; Dried Beans concentrate it, making the presence denser without taking up more space.

The Aged Chickpea's challenge is the depth mistaken for surface. A Bean who fits everywhere is often assumed to be shallow. These Beans must find ways to make their substance legible before it is looked for.
