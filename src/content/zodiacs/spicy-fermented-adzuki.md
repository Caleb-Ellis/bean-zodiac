Kimchi Adzuki Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most provocative — they emerge from the dark to rearrange the conversation.

Adzuki Beans know how to mark a moment; Spicy Beans insist on full attention and transform undeniably; Fermented Beans work the combination in the dark until something emerges that no one else produced by the same route.

The Kimchi Adzuki Bean's challenge is remembering provocation is a beginning. They must stay present for what comes after, because that is usually the conversation they meant to start.
