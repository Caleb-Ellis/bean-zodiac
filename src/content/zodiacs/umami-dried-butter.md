Dried Beans born in the Year of the Umami Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most mellow — the warmth is deep and slow, and nothing about their presence is ever in a hurry.

Butter Beans become extraordinary through warmth and time; Umami Beans build depth through patience; Dried Beans strip away the softness without losing the warmth that was always underneath.

The Dried Umami Butter Bean's challenge is movement when movement is needed. Mellowness is a virtue — and also, sometimes, what happens when inertia goes unexamined. These Beans must distinguish between slowness and stopping.
