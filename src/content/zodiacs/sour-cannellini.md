People born in the Year of the Sour Cannellini are the Bean Zodiac's most incisive editors. They have the Cannellini's natural elegance and taste, combined with a clarifying instinct that cuts away everything unnecessary with the clean efficiency of good lemon juice over a white bean. They are not ruthless; they are precise. They know what belongs in the bowl and what is just taking up space, and they are constitutionally unable to leave the unnecessary thing in. This is not always comfortable for the people around them, but the results — in everything they touch — are always cleaner and more worth eating.
