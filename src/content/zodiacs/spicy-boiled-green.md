Boiled Beans born in the Year of the Spicy Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most zealous — they have a cause and the heat to see it through.

Green Beans arrive enthusiastic and ready to begin; Spicy Beans turn that enthusiasm into urgent, demanding heat; Boiled Beans sustain the conviction in a long, patient simmer.

The Boiled Spicy Green Bean's challenge is allowing others to arrive differently. They must learn that zeal without room for dissent becomes pressure.
