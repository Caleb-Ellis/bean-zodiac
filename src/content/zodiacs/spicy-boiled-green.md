Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Boiled Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most zealous — they have a cause and the steady heat to keep driving it forward, long past the point where others have lost the thread.

Green Bean arrives already enthusiastic and ready to begin; Spicy turns that enthusiasm into an urgent, demanding heat; Boiled is what makes it last — the same composure that sustains a long simmer, directed entirely at whatever this Bean believes in. These are not flash-fire converts. The conviction has been simmering for a while.

The Spicy Boiled Green Bean's challenge is the Beans who do not share their fervour. Zeal without room for dissent becomes pressure. These Beans must allow others to arrive by their own path.
