People born in the Year of the Sweet Cannellini are the Bean Zodiac's most graceful — high-standards people who are, slowly and sometimes reluctantly, learning to enjoy things without improving them first. They have the Cannellini's elegant sensibility, and the sweetness in their character makes them genuinely capable of delight — when they allow it. The table they have spent so long carefully setting is for sitting at.

The Sweet Cannellini's challenge is sitting at it — and staying seated.
