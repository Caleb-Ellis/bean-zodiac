People born in the Year of the Sweet Cannellini are the high-standards people who are, slowly and sometimes reluctantly, learning to enjoy things without improving them first. They have the Cannellini's elegant sensibility and exacting taste, and the sweetness in their character makes them genuinely capable of delight — when they allow it. Their complication is the impulse to refine whatever is placed in front of them, including moments that were already good. The table they have spent so long carefully setting is for sitting at. The people who love them are waiting for them to actually sit down.
