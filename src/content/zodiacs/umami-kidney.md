People born in the Year of the Umami Kidney Bean are the great endurers — the ones who show up consistently, do the work without announcement, and arrive somewhere genuinely rich through the slow accumulation of showing up rather than through a single decisive moment. They have the Kidney Bean's earthy vitality and depth alongside a Umami richness that makes their endurance taste like something rather than simply costing something. They are good at the long game in a way that is genuinely admirable. What they are learning is that arriving is different from continuing, and that they have earned the right to put down the pot and serve what is inside.
