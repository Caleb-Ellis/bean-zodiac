Beans born in the Boiled Season of the Bitter Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most inquisitive — they follow threads until they lead somewhere unexpected.

Green Bean's energy produces constant questions; Bitter Beans strip the casual from the curious; Boiled Beans sit with questions long enough for real answers.

The Boiled Bitter Green Bean's challenge is the question that never closes. Inquiry without resolution is avoidance dressed as curiosity. They must occasionally allow themselves to arrive.
