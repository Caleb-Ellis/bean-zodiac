Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most artistic — not decorative, but genuinely making something that could not have existed without them.

Pinto Bean comes already painted, expression a condition of its existence rather than a choice; Bitter means the work does not yield its meaning at first glance — it demands something from whoever encounters it; Fermented takes the whole thing inward and transforms it privately until what emerges bears little resemblance to what went in. The result is genuinely original. It took a while, and it shows.

The Bitter Fermented Pinto Bean's challenge is the audience. Art made entirely in private runs out of material. These Beans need friction — not approval, but the resistance that keeps the work honest.
