Miso Pinto Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most apathetic — feeling has become, for now, exhausting.

Pinto Beans began vivid with expressive interiors; Umami Beans steep them so deeply richness becomes hard to distinguish from saturation; Fermented Beans take it inward until quiet emerges.

The Miso Pinto Bean's challenge is re-entry. They must find the one thing that still moves them and follow it back in.
