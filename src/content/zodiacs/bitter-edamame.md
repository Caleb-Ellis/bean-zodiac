People born in the Year of the Bitter Edamame are bright and full of genuine potential, and they are learning — sometimes impatiently — that not everything worth having arrives when expected. They have the Edamame's fresh energy and natural luminosity, but the Bitter Flavour in their character keeps asking them to sit with something that does not immediately taste like a win. They are not slower than other people; they are maturing more thoroughly. The complexity they are developing in the difficult seasons is exactly what will make them extraordinary later. They just have to resist the urge to pop out of the pod too soon.
