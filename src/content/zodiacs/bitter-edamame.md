People born in the Year of the Bitter Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most focused — practical and direct, but with a Bitter discernment that makes them very particular about where that efficiency is actually applied.

They carry the Edamame's refusal to waste a move, refined by a sharpness that distinguishes work worth doing from work that merely looks urgent. They are learning that doing less, more carefully, gets further than doing everything quickly.

The Bitter Edamame's challenge is applying the same discernment about where to spend their energy to the question of when to rest.
