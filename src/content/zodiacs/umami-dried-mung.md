Dried Beans born in the Year of the Umami Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most restorative — the care is deep and patient and works slowly, and it has never failed to work.

Mung Beans restore what has been depleted; Umami Beans reward those who stay long enough to receive the full depth; Dried Beans concentrate the instinct, making the effect more significant than the size of the Bean might suggest.

The Dried Umami Mung Bean's challenge is the cost of restoring everything around them. These Beans give until others are whole — and do not always notice that the same care has not been given to themselves. They must restore inward as well as outward.
