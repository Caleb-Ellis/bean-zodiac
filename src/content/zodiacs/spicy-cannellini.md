People born in the Year of the Spicy Cannellini are the elegant people who also, unexpectedly, have a streak of wildness — and who are slowly discovering that these two qualities are not in contradiction. They have the Cannellini's high standards and refined sensibility alongside a disruptive Spicy heat that occasionally catches everyone, including themselves, off guard. They are the most interesting at dinner parties — composed and articulate and then suddenly, genuinely surprising. They have learned, sometimes the hard way, that the best versions of their life did not follow the carefully curated plan.
