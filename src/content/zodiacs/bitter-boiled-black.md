Beans born in the Boiled Season of the Bitter Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most measured — not through restraint learned, but through a nature that has never needed to speak before it is certain.

Black Beans give up their richness slowly; Bitter Beans strip away anything unearned; Boiled Beans work in silence.

The Boiled Bitter Black Bean's challenge is legibility. Composure held too long becomes opacity, and opacity looks a great deal like coldness. These Beans must let some of the broth show before the dish is finished.
