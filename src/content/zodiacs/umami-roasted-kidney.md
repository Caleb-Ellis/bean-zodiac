Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most extravagant — they believe that the full experience is always better than the restrained one, and they commit to this belief completely.

Umami Roasted Kidney Beans go all the way in. Kidney Bean gives up its richness slowly and fully, colouring everything it touches; Umami adds depth and substance, the quality that makes something feel like it was worth the wait; Roasted coaxes all of this outward and fills the room — the warmth, the colour, the announcement of presence. These Beans do not sample. They commit. And they bring everyone around them into the commitment.

The Umami Roasted Kidney Bean's challenge is learning that abundance and excess are not the same thing. Extravagance in service of genuine depth is one thing; extravagance as a substitute for it is another. These Beans must know which one they are practising when the room gets very full.
