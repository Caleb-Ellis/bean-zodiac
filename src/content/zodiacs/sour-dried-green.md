Dried Beans born in the Year of the Sour Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most bracing — the freshness remains, but it is cold and sharp and entirely without cushion.

Green Beans arrive with urgency and optimism; Sour Beans strip away what softens the impact; Dried Beans hold it in concentrated form, without the warmth that once rounded the edge.

The Dried Sour Green Bean's challenge is the effect on those who are not ready. Bracing is useful for those who need waking — it is another thing for those already struggling to stand. These Beans must read the room before delivering the shock.
