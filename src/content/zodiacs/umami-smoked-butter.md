Burnished Butter Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most languid — the ease and the depth have found each other and have no pressing business elsewhere.

Butter Beans become extraordinary simply by being given enough time; Umami Beans reward exactly that slowness with a richness that cannot be rushed; Smoked Beans let the quality drift through the room from somewhere comfortable and unhurried.

The Burnished Butter Bean's challenge is motion. Languid is a tempo, not a destination. These Beans must find at least one thing they are moving toward — slowly is fine.
