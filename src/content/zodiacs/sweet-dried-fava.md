Crystallised Fava Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most indomitable — generosity as a force, courage concentrated until nothing can stop it.

Fava Beans move toward difficulty and don't stop; Sweet Beans extend warmth without requiring conditions; Dried Beans concentrate both until what remains is inexhaustible.

The Crystallised Fava Bean's challenge is the toll of carrying so much. These Beans are generous and relentless and rarely ask for support themselves. They must learn that being indomitable does not mean carrying it all alone.
