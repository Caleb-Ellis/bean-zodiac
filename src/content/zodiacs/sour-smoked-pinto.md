Smoked Beans born in the Year of the Sour Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most sardonic — their expressiveness has curdled into something that watches the proceedings from a slight remove.

Pinto Beans carry vivid complexity that colours the whole pot; Sour Beans give that colour an acidic quality; Smoked Beans release it into the room without quite owning it.

The Smoked Sour Pinto Bean's challenge is sincerity. Sardony is a mode, not a position. These Beans must find something they will say plainly, without the distance.
