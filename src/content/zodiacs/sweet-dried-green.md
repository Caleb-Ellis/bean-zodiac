Crystallized Green Beans are the Bean Zodiac's most perennial — the freshness never depletes; the energy renews before it runs out.

Green Beans return before the season ends; Sweet Beans carry warmth without effort; Dried Beans concentrate it into something lasting, carried across seasons without diminishing.

The Crystallized Green Bean's challenge is the expectation of perpetual renewal. These Beans can sustain much — but they are not inexhaustible, and those around them may not know that. They must allow others to see the effort behind the ease.
