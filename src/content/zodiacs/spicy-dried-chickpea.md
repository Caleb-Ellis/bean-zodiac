Dried Beans born in the Year of the Spicy Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most combustible — they fit into every room, and in every room, eventually, something ignites.

Chickpeas adapt to any environment; Spicy Beans make the energy immediate and hard to contain; Dried Beans concentrate it, so even small contact produces significant heat.

The Dried Spicy Chickpea's challenge is the fire they started but didn't plan. These Beans move through the world freely and set things off without always intending to. They must learn to notice what they are leaving behind them.
