Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most tolerant — they hold space for what is unlike them with a warmth that goes beyond politeness, because they have encountered enough difference to know that difference is usually worth staying for.

Chickpea has made itself at home in every cuisine it has entered, and arrived at genuine appreciation for the variety; Umami provides the depth that allows this to be more than mere acceptance — these Beans are not merely tolerant, they are interested; Roasted fills the space with conviviality, making the tolerance feel like an invitation rather than a concession. These Beans have simply been around long enough to lose their certainty about what doesn't belong.

The Umami Roasted Chickpea's challenge is the limit. Tolerance that extends without any boundary is not tolerance but an absence of position. These Beans must know what they actually will not accept — and hold that line, warmly, without apology.
