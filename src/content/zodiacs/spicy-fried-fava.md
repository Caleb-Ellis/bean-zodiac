Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fried Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most risk-taking — they commit before the math is finished, enter before the temperature is confirmed, and make it work on the way through.

Fava Bean knows that the best things require the willingness to start before conditions are ideal; Spicy means the risk is urgent, demanding — not a calculated hedge but a full commitment at full heat; Fried makes it immediate and irreversible, no room to reconsider once the thing has begun. What comes out on the other side is either transformed or ruined. These Beans are fine with that.

The Spicy Fried Fava Bean's challenge is that tolerance for risk is not the same as immunity to its consequences. These Beans must build the habit of looking back — not to second-guess, but to learn which of their instincts are reliable.
