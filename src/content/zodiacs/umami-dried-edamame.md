Dried Beans born in the Year of the Umami Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most foundational — they are the thing under the thing, holding the structure without requiring credit for it.

Edamame Beans become whatever is needed; Umami Beans build depth slowly and without announcement; Dried Beans strip away what is secondary, leaving only the essential load-bearing.

The Dried Umami Edamame's challenge is recognition withheld. A foundation does its best work invisibly — which means those who rest on it often don't know it's there. These Beans must decide whether invisibility serves them, or whether it's time to be seen.
