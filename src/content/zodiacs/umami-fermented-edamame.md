Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fermented Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most observant — they notice what others have already looked at without seeing, and they hold what they notice for a long time before deciding what to do with it.

Edamame is already reading the room before it arrives; Umami means the observation has depth — not just what is visible but what the visible thing implies; Fermented lets the impression work in private, turning it over until it becomes understanding rather than just a noticed fact. These Beans rarely say what they have seen until they know why it matters.

The Umami Fermented Edamame's challenge is the cost of private processing. Observations held too long stop being useful to anyone else. These Beans must share what they are seeing, and sometimes sooner rather than later.
