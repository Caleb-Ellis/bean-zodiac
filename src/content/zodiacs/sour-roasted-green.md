Beans born in the Season of the Sour Roasted Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most cheerful — a warmth that is hard to dismiss because it is also honest, and a brightness that has already acknowledged the difficulty before arriving with a smile.

Green Bean's optimism is energetic and immediate; Roasted fills the room with it freely; Sour is the unexpected ingredient — it keeps the cheerfulness from cloying, sharpening the warmth into something more durable than pleasantness. These Beans are not naive. They have taken account of the hard part and are choosing good humour anyway.

The Sour Roasted Green Bean's challenge is being taken seriously. Cheerfulness is easily mistaken for shallowness. These Beans know more than they appear to, and must occasionally let the full picture show.
