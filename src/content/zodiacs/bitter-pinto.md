People born in the Year of the Bitter Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most self-aware — vivid and expressive, but equipped with a Bitter self-knowledge that can see the flaws in their own work as clearly as anyone else's. They are not easy to categorise, and they have stopped trying to make themselves easier. Their complexity deepens over time, and the people who stay are exactly the ones they want.

The Bitter Pinto Bean's challenge is knowing when the self-assessment has finished its work — seeing your own flaws clearly is only useful until it becomes its own obstacle.
