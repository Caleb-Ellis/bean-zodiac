Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most clever — they have usually found the solution while others are still mapping the problem, and they have the grace not to make this uncomfortable.

Chickpea is resourceful by nature, finding the angle that wasn't obvious; Sour cuts immediately to what is precise, stripping away everything that doesn't carry information; Fermented does the real work privately, surfacing conclusions already fully formed. These Beans do not deliberate in public. What looks like quick wit is usually the tip of something much longer.

The Sour Fermented Chickpea's challenge is legibility. Clever Beans travel fast, and the route they took is not always visible to the people arriving at the same destination more slowly. These Beans must occasionally show the working — not to prove themselves, but to bring others along.
