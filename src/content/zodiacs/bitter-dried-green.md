Dried Beans born in the Year of the Bitter Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most honed — all the urgency, none of the waste; all the speed, only toward what deserves it.

Green Beans arrive fast and ready; Bitter Beans insist that arrival earn its place; Dried Beans strip away the restlessness, leaving only the edge.

The Dried Bitter Green Bean's challenge is the spark without tinder. Precision pointed at nothing is still wasted. These Beans must find something worthy of the sharpness they have cultivated.
