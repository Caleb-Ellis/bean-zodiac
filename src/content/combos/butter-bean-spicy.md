Butter-basted chilli, creamy bean stew with a serious amount of dried chilli stirred in — heat and richness have always worked together, and this year your characteristic warmth gets turned up rather than extinguished. Your patience will be tested and your comfort zone will get slightly uncomfortable, but you'll find that softness and intensity are not opposites. They were always the same dish, just at different temperatures. The spicy year needs someone grounded enough to carry the heat without losing the nourishment.

**Fortune for this year:** Turn up the heat. The creaminess can take it.
