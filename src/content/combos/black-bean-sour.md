Sour does for the black bean exactly what lime does for black bean soup — doesn't change what's there, just makes the whole thing crisper and more defined. The mystery you usually prefer is getting a little more legible this year, and honestly? Fine. Things you've kept vague will become specific. Opinions you've held privately will want to be said out loud. The depth is still there, the complexity is still there, it just has better lighting now and you can actually see where the furniture is.

**Fortune for this year:** Clarity is not your enemy. It just feels that way at first.
