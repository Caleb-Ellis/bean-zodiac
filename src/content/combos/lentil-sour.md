Lentils with tamarind, with preserved lemon, with a good vinegar — sourness has always been a natural partner for the lentil, sharpening the earthiness into something brighter and more vivid. Your already considerable clear-sightedness gets an extra edge this year. You will call things what they are. Some people will find this bracing. Others will find it a relief. Both reactions are correct, and neither is your problem. You've been keeping the accurate assessment to yourself for a while. This year, say it.

**Fortune for this year:** You've always known. You just didn't always say it.
