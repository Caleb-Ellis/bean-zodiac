tagline: Gentle, but don't whisper when someone needs to hear you.
---

Spicy mung bean is a productive confrontation — the mung bean's cooling, gentle nature meets heat's insistence on being felt, and the result is warming and restorative rather than aggressive. There's useful friction here. Your instinct toward gentle restoration is correct, but this year it's asked to be more direct. You can be soothing and clear at the same time. You can heal something and also tell the truth about what caused it. Don't whisper when someone needs to actually hear you.

**Fortune for this year:** You can restore gently and still mean it.
