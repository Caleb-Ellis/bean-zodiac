The pinto's speckled complexity meets bitter's insistence that good things take time to appreciate — and honestly, the combination makes total sense. You've never been the most immediately legible bean, and bitter is the year that stops apologising for that. Your individuality deepens from colourful to genuinely complex this year, which means the right people will find you more interesting than ever, and the wrong people will continue to prefer something simpler. That's fine. Let the complexity accumulate.

**Fortune for this year:** Not everyone has developed the palate for you yet. Give them a season.
