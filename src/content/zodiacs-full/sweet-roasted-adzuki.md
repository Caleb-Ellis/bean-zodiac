Beans born in the Season of the Roasted Sweet Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most generous — the Adzuki's gift for celebration, Sweet's effortless warmth, and Roasted's radiant heat combine into a Bean who gives with a completeness that others return to.

Adzuki Bean is the ingredient of lucky occasions, the red that tints the rice and marks the auspicious day; Sweet makes this uncomplicated — no calculation, no conditions; Roasted coaxes the sugars out until the room is full of something irresistible. These Beans give without strategy, the way a roasting bean fills the whole kitchen before it has been asked to.

The Roasted Sweet Adzuki Bean's challenge is allowing generosity to flow both ways. A generosity that cannot be returned creates distance rather than closeness. These Beans must occasionally let someone bring the red bean paste, and eat it without trying to top it.
