Beans born in the Season of the Sour Boiled Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most righteous — they know what is fair, and they will wait as long as it takes for the situation to admit it.

Fava Bean doesn't shrink from what requires effort; Sour sharpens the principle to a clear edge, stripping away the comfort that might otherwise make compromise feel acceptable; Boiled means this isn't a flash of indignation but a sustained position, held patiently until the situation resolves or the principle is acknowledged. These Beans do not give up and they do not cool down.

The Sour Boiled Fava Bean's challenge is the difference between righteousness and inflexibility. Not every situation has a moral axis. These Beans must be careful not to apply the standard of principle to problems that are simply complicated.
