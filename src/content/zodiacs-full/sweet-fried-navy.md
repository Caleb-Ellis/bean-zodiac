Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fried Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most romantic — they commit to people with the same absolute loyalty they bring to everything else, and they mean it completely.

Navy Bean is loyal by nature — it stays, it sustains, it is still there when other things have gone; Sweet opens the whole arrangement into something generous and joyful, capable of real tenderness; Fried delivers the feeling at full intensity, without patience for half-measures or hedging. These Beans fall fully. When they love something, it is not a mood — it is a position.

The Sweet Fried Navy Bean's challenge is the gap between the beloved and the ideal. Navy Bean loyalty, once given, holds hard — and the person it was given to will not always match the image. These Beans must love the actual Bean in front of them, not only the perfect version.
