Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fermented Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most compulsive — they return to certain things with a regularity that puzzles them, drawn back before they have finished leaving.

Mung Bean has a deep pull toward the things it tends; Spicy makes that pull feel urgent, carrying a heat that is hard to argue with; Fermented turns it inward, where the loop runs privately, out of others' sight. Spicy Fermented Mung Beans know when they are in the loop. This does not always help. The mechanism does not require consent.

The Spicy Fermented Mung Bean's challenge is interruption. These Beans must find the moment of gap between the urge and the return, and do something different in it — not to end the compulsion, but to discover they can.
