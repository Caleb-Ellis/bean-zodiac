Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fried Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most blunt — not unkind by intention, but unwilling to spend breath on softening what can simply be said.

Edamame doesn't believe in preamble; Sour won't allow truth to be diluted for comfort; Fried means it arrives before tact could intervene. These Beans say the thing, and then look mildly surprised that this is notable.

The Sour Fried Edamame's challenge is the gap between saying true and saying useful. A fact delivered without context is sometimes just a fact. These Beans must ask whether the truth is being given or simply discharged.
