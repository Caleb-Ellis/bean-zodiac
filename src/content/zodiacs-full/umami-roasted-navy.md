Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most wistful — they carry the warmth of what has already passed, and they carry it faithfully.

Navy Bean is loyal by nature, and loyalty directed at the past is memory that will not let go; Umami gives the recollection its depth — not nostalgia for a surface but a genuine richness that accumulated over time and cannot be replicated; Roasted holds the whole arrangement in a warmth that makes the past feel present, not as longing but as company. These Beans are not stuck. They are keeping faith with something real.

The Umami Roasted Navy Bean's challenge is the present that gets missed while tending the past. The warmth is genuine, the loyalty is real — but the current room also contains something worth attending to. These Beans must occasionally let the past stay where it is.
