Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fried Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most serious — they bring the same gravity to small things as to large ones, because they have decided the distinction is not always worth making.

Mung Bean understands that what looks ordinary can conceal something vivid and important; Umami adds depth to everything it touches, refusing lightness; Fried means there is no slow build — the seriousness arrives immediately and in full. Umami Fried Mung Beans do not switch registers easily. Levity is not their native frequency. What they offer instead is complete attention, and that is not a lesser thing.

The Umami Fried Mung Bean's challenge is proportion. These Beans must occasionally let something be small, and let themselves be in it without their full weight.
