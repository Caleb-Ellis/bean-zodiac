Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most bighearted — the generosity is not a quality they exercise but a quality they are, and it extends outward without diminishing, like a warmth that seems to have no source it is depleting.

Chickpea is the zodiac's connector, at ease in any company and genuinely interested in all of it; Sweet makes everything more open, freely given, a generosity that does not track the exchange; Roasted fills the room with it, carrying the warmth to the edges and making an occasion out of whatever is available. These Beans give first and account later, if at all.

The Sweet Roasted Chickpea's challenge is the cost. Even a capacious heart has limits, and bighearted Beans are the last to notice when they have reached them. These Beans must learn to receive as readily as they give.
