Beans born in the Season of the Fried Spicy Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most ambitious — they have identified the larger thing and they are already moving toward it, at heat, without waiting for a safer moment to begin.

Cannellini Bean holds a high standard and applies it to everything, including what it is willing to aim for; Spicy means the pursuit is full-temperature, total — not a considered aspiration but an urgent demand that the better thing be reached; Fried delivers on this with decisiveness and immediate momentum, no deliberation, no revisiting, the oil already hot by the time the question has finished being asked. These Beans do not mistake motion for progress, because Cannellini's instinct for refinement keeps the aim honest. They are going somewhere specific.

The Fried Spicy Cannellini Bean's challenge is peripheral vision. Beans moving at this speed and with this focus leave things in their wake — opportunities, relationships, obligations. These Beans must occasionally slow down long enough to see what is alongside them.
