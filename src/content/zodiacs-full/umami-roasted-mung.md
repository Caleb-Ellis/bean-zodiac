Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most melancholy — not unhappy, but given to a depth of feeling that keeps one foot in what has passed and what will not come back.

Mung Bean absorbs the conditions around it, including loss; Umami means nothing is shallow, every experience leaving a residue that does not quickly clear; Roasted holds it all at a steady warmth that is neither cold enough to forget nor hot enough to burn off. Umami Roasted Mung Beans feel things thoroughly. They remember the specific quality of certain evenings. They grieve carefully and for longer than the world asks.

The Umami Roasted Mung Bean's challenge is the present tense. Melancholy is beautiful and accurate and can become a place to live. These Beans must let now be enough, even when it does not have the weight of what came before.
