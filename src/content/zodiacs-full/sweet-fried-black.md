Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fried Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most driven — not from cold ambition or fear of stillness, but from a warmth and determination that moves outward and tends to pull others along without meaning to.

Black Bean is determined and does not give anything up easily; Sweet means the forward movement comes with generosity attached, a quality of wanting others to arrive with them rather than arriving alone; Fried means all of this happens fast, with the kind of decisive energy that doesn't wait for consensus before it starts moving.

The Sweet Fried Black Bean's challenge is slowing for the ones they're pulling. Speed that outruns the group is not drive — it is departure. These Beans must check behind them sometimes, not to slow down, but to ensure the warmth is reaching the people it was meant for.
