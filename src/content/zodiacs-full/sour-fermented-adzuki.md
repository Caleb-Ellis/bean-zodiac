Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most self-aware — they have taken the Adzuki's joyful instincts and avoidant shadow through a long internal reckoning, and what has emerged is an unsentimental picture of themselves.

Adzuki Bean knows how to celebrate and knows how to avoid; Sour cuts through both, making everything more legible; Fermented takes this inward and works on it until there is nothing left that hasn't been examined. These Beans know when they're celebrating because something deserves it, and when they're celebrating to avoid something else — and they can usually tell the difference, which is the whole thing.

The Sour Fermented Adzuki Bean's challenge is not mistaking self-knowledge for self-acceptance. These Beans must occasionally put the analysis down and simply be in what they know — without commentary.
