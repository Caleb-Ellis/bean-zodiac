Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Roasted Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most self-reliant — they have learned to supply what they need from their own resources, and the habit has held.

Navy Bean is principled and capable, the kind of presence that thickens every pot without being asked; Spicy has already provided the heat for transformation — these Beans have been through things and emerged from them intact; Roasted holds the result in a warmth that is turned mostly inward, sustaining the self before extending outward. These Beans don't wait for conditions to improve. They make do, and then some.

The Spicy Roasted Navy Bean's challenge is receiving. Self-reliance that becomes reflexive can look like strength but function as refusal — the help that was warranted, the support that was genuinely available, all turned away by habit. These Beans must notice when self-reliance has stopped being a virtue and become a default.
