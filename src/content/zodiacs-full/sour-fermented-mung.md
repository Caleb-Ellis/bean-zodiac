Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fermented Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most insecure — they have developed, through long interior processing, an exquisitely detailed understanding of everything they might be getting wrong.

Mung Bean is already prone to doubt — the bean that gives freely worries it is giving wrong; Sour sharpens that perception until every gap and flaw is in perfect focus; Fermented ensures all of this happens out of sight, where it has time to become elaborate. The result is a Bean of genuine sensitivity and occasional paralysis. They are also, frequently, exactly right about the thing they were worried about.

The Sour Fermented Mung Bean's challenge is proportion. Not every detected flaw is a crisis. These Beans must find the difference between the signal worth attending to and the noise the process generates on its own.
