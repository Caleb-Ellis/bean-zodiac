Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most scholarly — they have a relationship with knowledge that is long, devoted, and not particularly concerned with whether anyone else finds it convenient.

Cannellini Bean refines and elevates everything it accompanies, including ideas; Bitter favours the complex, the thing that rewards sustained attention; Fermented works the investment in the dark, producing something with the density of a conclusion arrived at through genuine study. These Beans have followed the footnote. When they surface an opinion, it has citations.

The Bitter Fermented Cannellini Bean's challenge is translation. Scholarship held entirely within is just a very organised interior. These Beans must occasionally find the form that allows what they know to become useful — not just to themselves, but to the room.
