Beans born in the Season of the Fermented Bitter Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most scholarly — they have a relationship with knowledge that is long, devoted, and not particularly concerned with whether anyone else finds it convenient.

Cannellini Bean refines and elevates everything it accompanies, including ideas; Bitter favours the complex, the acquired, the thing that rewards sustained attention; Fermented takes this investment and works it in the dark, on its own schedule, producing something that has the specific density of a conclusion arrived at through genuine study rather than casual reading. These Beans have followed the footnote. They have read the original. When they surface an opinion, it has citations.

The Fermented Bitter Cannellini Bean's challenge is translation. Scholarship held entirely within is just a very organised interior. These Beans must occasionally find the form that allows what they know to become useful — not just to themselves, but to the room.
