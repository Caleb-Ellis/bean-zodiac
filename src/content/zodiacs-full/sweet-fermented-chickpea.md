Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fermented Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most empathetic — they feel the texture of what someone else is carrying, often before that person has named it to themselves, and this is not a skill they learned but a way they are built.

Chickpea is the zodiac's natural translator, moving between temperaments and finding common ground as a matter of instinct; Sweet ensures the attention is warm and genuinely given; Fermented takes the whole operation internal, working quietly until what emerges is understanding rather than just recognition. The person being seen tends to feel it regardless.

The Sweet Fermented Chickpea's challenge is their own interior. Beans who absorb the emotional lives of others so readily sometimes lose track of where the other person ends and they begin. These Beans must know what is theirs before they take on what belongs to someone else.
