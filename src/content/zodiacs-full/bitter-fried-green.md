Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fried Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most excitable — they bring all of Green Bean's enthusiasm at full volume, with Fried's heat behind it, barely held in check by a Bitter clarity that knows exactly what is worth it.

Green Bean already moves fast and wants to; Fried means there is no interval between impulse and action; Bitter, somewhat improbably, provides the direction — not dampening the energy but pointing it at something real. The excitement these Beans feel is not performed. It arrives already at operating temperature.

The Bitter Fried Green Bean's challenge is the thing that isn't worth it. Most things pass Bitter's filter; a few don't, and these Beans must trust that verdict. Not every open door deserves the full sprint.
