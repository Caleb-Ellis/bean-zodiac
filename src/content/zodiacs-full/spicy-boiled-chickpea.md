Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Boiled Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most resourceful — they work with what is available, and what is available is almost always enough, because these Beans have a gift for seeing what can be done with it.

Chickpea has made itself at home in every kitchen it has entered, not by waiting for ideal conditions but by finding what the present conditions can produce; Spicy means this is urgent — something adequate must be produced now, from these ingredients, without delay; Boiled sustains the operation with patience, releasing what it contains over time. These Beans do not hold out for the right tools. They look at the tools in front of them and start.

The Spicy Boiled Chickpea's challenge is asking for help. Resourceful Beans become so practised at solving problems alone that they stop considering whether a problem was meant to be solved alone. These Beans must notice when the right resource is another Bean.
