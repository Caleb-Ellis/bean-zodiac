Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Roasted Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most pioneering — drawn toward the unmapped territory not in spite of knowing the risks, but because knowing the risks and going anyway is the only kind of movement that matters to them.

Fava Bean requires effort and returns something no simpler alternative provides; Bitter demands that the effort point somewhere genuinely worth pointing; Roasted means the whole enterprise radiates outward with a warmth that brings others into what would otherwise be a solitary advance. These Beans don't just arrive somewhere first — they make the arriving feel like an occasion.
