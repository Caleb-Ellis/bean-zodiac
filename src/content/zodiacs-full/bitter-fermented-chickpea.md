Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most collaborative — they understand that what is made together can be better than what either party would have produced alone, and they have arranged their working life around this understanding.

Chickpea is the zodiac's great connector, finding common ground between temperaments as a matter of instinct; Bitter adds the discernment to know which collaborations are worth the investment; Fermented takes the work somewhere private, where transformation happens without performance. These Beans do not collaborate to be seen doing it. What comes out the other side is genuinely different from what either party brought in.

The Bitter Fermented Chickpea's challenge is the solo task. Not everything can be done with others, and some things are done worse for trying. These Beans must make peace with the work that only they can finish.
