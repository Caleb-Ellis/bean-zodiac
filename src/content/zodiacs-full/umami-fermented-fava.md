Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fermented Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most rebellious — they resist what they have been handed not as a reflex but as a conclusion, reached slowly and privately after a long examination of what the existing arrangement is actually for.

Fava Bean does not accept difficulty as a reason to stop; Umami means the rebellion has depth — not a surface rejection but a thoroughly developed alternative; Fermented means it has been worked on in private for a long time, converting the existing conditions into something new through a process no one else was monitoring. These Beans don't revolt loudly. They arrive somewhere the rest of the room wasn't expecting.

The Umami Fermented Fava Bean's challenge is legibility. Rebellion that no one can follow is its own kind of enclosure. These Beans must surface enough of their reasoning that others can decide whether they want to follow — not to court permission, but to allow the possibility.
