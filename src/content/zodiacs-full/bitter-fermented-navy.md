Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most bookish — they have built their understanding from deep reading, and they carry it with them as a private architecture no one else can see.

Navy Bean is loyal to systems; Bitter means the systems it keeps are the ones that have genuinely earned their place — no inherited opinions, no unexamined conclusions; Fermented does the whole thing in private, converting information into knowledge through a process that requires no audience. What emerges is a mind stocked with things that other minds haven't reached.

The Bitter Fermented Navy Bean's challenge is translation. The private library is substantial — but knowledge that remains untransmitted cannot do anything in the world. These Beans must occasionally bring the book outside.
