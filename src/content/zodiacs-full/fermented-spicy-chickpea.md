Beans born in the Season of the Fermented Spicy Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most offbeat — they have the Chickpea's gift for connecting with anyone, but the ground they find tends to be somewhere unexpected.

Fermented Spicy Chickpeas don't plan to be strange. Chickpea begins as the zodiac's most universal Bean, at home in every kitchen and every company; Spicy introduces a heat that demands real transformation, not just accommodation; Fermented takes what remains and works on it in the dark until something emerges that wasn't there before. These Beans land in rooms easily and leave them slightly altered. The warmth is genuine. The weird is not performed.

The Fermented Spicy Chickpea's challenge is not losing the thread back. Fermentation can go somewhere no one else can follow. These Beans must hold onto the warmth — it is what makes the strangeness worth staying for.
