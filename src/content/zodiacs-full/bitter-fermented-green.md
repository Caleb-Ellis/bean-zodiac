Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fermented Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most imaginative — they can see something that isn't there yet and render it precise enough that others can see it too.

Green Bean generates ideas fast and keeps generating them; Bitter edits ruthlessly, discarding what is only decorative; Fermented works the remainder in private, converting raw energy into something richer and stranger than what entered. When these Beans produce an idea, it has already been tested against itself.

The Bitter Fermented Green Bean's challenge is letting others in early. An imagination that runs entirely in private sometimes arrives with a vision no one has been prepared for. These Beans must share the draft, not only the finished thing.
