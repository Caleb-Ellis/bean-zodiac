Beans born in the Season of the Fermented Sweet Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most sentimental — they feel the weight of occasions and people with a completeness that is difficult to explain but impossible to miss.

Adzuki Bean marks the auspicious moments; Sweet keeps this generous and yielding; Fermented holds the combination in the dark until what emerges has the complexity of something genuinely kept, not just remembered. These Beans find that small occasions move them profoundly, that leaving a place feels like a ceremony, that a familiar meal can carry the weight of an entire year.

The Fermented Sweet Adzuki Bean's challenge is not apologising for feeling. These Beans often preface their sentiment with a caveat — I know it's nothing, but — and the caveat is the only dishonest part. These Beans must learn to offer the feeling without the disclaimer.
