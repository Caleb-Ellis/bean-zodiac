Beans born in the Season of the Sour Boiled Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most literal-minded — what is said is what is meant, what is meant is what was said, and the space between those two things is not where they live.

Navy Bean is principled and precise — it knows what is required and does it, exactly; Sour strips everything to its actual shape, removing the decorative and the approximate; Boiled steeps the whole arrangement until nothing floats above the surface anymore — no metaphor, no implication, no subtext. These Beans are not missing the point. They have decided the point is what the sentence says.

The Sour Boiled Navy Bean's challenge is meaning that cannot be stated plainly. Some things are not said because they are not sayable — and that is still information. These Beans must allow that precision can sometimes miss what it is measuring.
