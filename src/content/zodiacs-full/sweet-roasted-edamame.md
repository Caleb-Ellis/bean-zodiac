Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most diligent — they do the work carefully and completely, not because they are perfectionists but because carelessness offends them on a practical level.

Edamame counts every step and makes each one count; Sweet means the effort is warm, not grinding, genuinely given; Roasted holds everything at a steady heat that coaxes the work toward something done rather than something abandoned. These Beans finish.

The Sweet Roasted Edamame's challenge is recognising when the work is good enough. Diligence without a limit has the same shape as avoidance. These Beans must trust the finished thing and let it go.
