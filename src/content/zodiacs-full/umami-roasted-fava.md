Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most defiant — they refuse what has been decided for them, and they do this with a warmth and fullness that makes it clear the refusal is not a wound but a position.

Fava Bean requires that you do the double work to find what's actually inside; Umami gives the defiance its depth, so it is not a reactive no but something rich and accumulated, built from a long time of noticing what doesn't fit; Roasted fills the room with it — the defiance is warm, even generous, the kind that invites rather than isolates. These Beans don't resent what was handed to them. They simply decline it.

The Umami Roasted Fava Bean's challenge is the ongoing cost of refusing the given path. Defiance maintained over a long stretch requires its own sustenance. These Beans must make sure they are nourished by what they are building, not only by what they are refusing.
