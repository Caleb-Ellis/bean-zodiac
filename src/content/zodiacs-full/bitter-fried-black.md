Black Bean has been watching, weighing, processing long before it speaks; Bitter refuses anything that hasn't genuinely earned its place; Fried delivers the result instantly, at heat, with no deliberation visible in the output. The Fried Bitter Black Bean's challenge is the effect of speed on the recipient. Not everyone wants to be cut to the truth before they have had time to prepare. These Beans must decide when to lead with the answer and when to let the question finish forming.
