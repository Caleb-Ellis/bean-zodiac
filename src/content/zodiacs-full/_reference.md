Beans born in the Season of the Fried Umami Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most keen — not eager in the restless sense, but sharp in the way that comes from having absorbed a great deal and learned to read it fast.

Fried Umami Edamame don't deliberate — they already know. Edamame is efficient by nature, cutting to what's needed; Umami provides the depth to know what that is; Fried locks it in hot, with no room for second-guessing. What looks like instinct is actually rigour at speed. These Beans have done the reading, and they respond to the world accordingly.

The Fried Umami Edamame's challenge is patience with those who haven't caught up. Not everyone processes at frying temperature. These Beans must trust that arriving somewhere together is sometimes the point.
