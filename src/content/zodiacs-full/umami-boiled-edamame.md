Beans born in the Season of the Umami Boiled Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most dedicated — they commit to a thing fully and they stay with it, not out of stubbornness but because they have learned that most worthwhile things only yield to time.

Edamame shows up completely and does the work asked of it; Umami gives the dedication its depth — not the bright early commitment but the kind that deepens the longer it runs; Boiled means the transformation is full and patient, nothing done halfway. These Beans are still there when the thing finally becomes what it was trying to be.

The Umami Boiled Edamame's challenge is knowing the difference between dedication and continuation past the point of return. Not every project deserves the whole stretch. These Beans must occasionally let themselves stop.
