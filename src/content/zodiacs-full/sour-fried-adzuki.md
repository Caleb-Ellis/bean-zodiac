Adzuki Bean wants to celebrate the people around it, to mark what they deserve; Sour insists that real celebration requires honesty; Fried means this arrives before there has been time to soften or decorate it. These Beans don't wait for a better moment, because they have learned that better moments are often just delays.

The Fried Sour Adzuki Bean's challenge is reading the room fast enough to match their own speed. Candour before trust has been established is just information, not connection. These Beans must sometimes wait one beat — not to soften the truth, but to ensure it lands somewhere it can be received.
