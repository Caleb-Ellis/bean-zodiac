Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Roasted Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most clingy — they love with an intensity that makes proximity feel essential, and they are always aware of when it is not there.

Mung Bean needs to give in order to feel connected; Spicy makes the connection feel urgent and impossible to dilute; Roasted holds everything warm, filling the space between two Beans with something it is genuinely uncomfortable to leave. Spicy Roasted Mung Beans do not want to crowd the Beans they love. They just need to know they are still there.

The Spicy Roasted Mung Bean's challenge is the space in between. These Beans must learn that absence is not departure, and that love sustained across distance is still love.
