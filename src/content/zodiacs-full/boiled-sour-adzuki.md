Beans born in the Season of the Boiled Sour Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most sincere — they do not mark occasions they don't mean, and when they mean one, there is nothing between the feeling and the expression.

Adzuki Bean's instinct is for celebration; Sour strips away anything performative, leaving only what is genuinely felt; Boiled refuses to surface before the feeling is real. When they celebrate you, the red tints everything — not because they planned an effect, but because they cannot help it when they mean it.

The Boiled Sour Adzuki Bean's challenge is trusting that sincerity is enough. Their joy is real but rarely decorated. These Beans must learn that meaning something fully, and saying so plainly, is the whole gift.
