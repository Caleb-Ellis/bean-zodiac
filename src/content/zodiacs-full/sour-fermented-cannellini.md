Beans born in the Season of the Fermented Sour Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most critical — not harsh, but constitutionally unable to look at something without knowing exactly how it could be better.

Fermented Sour Cannellini Beans process in private. Cannellini Bean already curates ruthlessly — its instinct is to refine, to remove, to elevate whatever it accompanies; Sour cuts through sentiment and softens nothing; Fermented takes this discernment inward and works on it until what emerges is not an opinion but a conclusion. These Beans have usually known for some time what they are about to tell you. They have simply been waiting until they were certain.

The Fermented Sour Cannellini Bean's challenge is delivery. A conclusion arrived at through long fermentation does not always land gently. These Beans must remember that precision without warmth is just sharpness — and the Cannellini Bean has always known how to add the olive oil.
