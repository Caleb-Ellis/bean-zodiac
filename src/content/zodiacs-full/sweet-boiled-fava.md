Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Boiled Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most persistent — they don't require things to be easy to continue doing them, and they don't require the end to be visible to keep walking toward it.

Fava Bean knows that most things worth having require sustained effort; Sweet gives the persistence a warmth that makes it feel less like endurance and more like commitment; Boiled is the long slow immersion that makes it real — not a burst of effort but a consistent presence that compounds. These Beans are still there when the thing finally becomes what it was trying to be.

The Sweet Boiled Fava Bean's challenge is distinguishing persistence from continuation past the point of return. Not every difficult thing deserves more time simply because time has already been spent. These Beans must be willing to stop when stopping is the right move, not only when the work is finished.
