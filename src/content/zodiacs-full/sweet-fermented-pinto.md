Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fermented Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most anxious — they have already run every scenario, and several of them did not go well.

Pinto Bean imagines prolifically — every outcome, every version, every possible marking on every possible map; Sweet wants each of them to go well, wants the people involved to be okay; Fermented takes all of this inward and processes it without a timeline, in the dark, thoroughly and without natural end. The work is complete. The work is also relentless.

The Sweet Fermented Pinto Bean's challenge is the revision cycle. There is a point at which more preparation becomes damage. These Beans must learn to stop at good enough and step into the room.
