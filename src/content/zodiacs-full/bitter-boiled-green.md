Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Boiled Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most inquisitive — they do not just notice the open door; they want to know who built it and why.

Green Bean's restless energy produces a constant flow of questions; Bitter demands each question be worth asking — it strips the casual from the curious; Boiled provides the patience to sit with a question long enough for a real answer to surface. These Beans are not interested in the obvious. They follow the thread until it leads somewhere the room didn't expect.

The Bitter Boiled Green Bean's challenge is the question that never closes. Inquiry without resolution is not understanding — it is avoidance dressed up as curiosity. These Beans must occasionally allow themselves to arrive.
