Beans born in the Season of the Fried Sour Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most meticulous — they do the thing correctly, at speed, and they notice when it has not been done correctly before anyone else has thought to look.

Cannellini Bean brings natural precision to everything it touches — its instinct is to refine, to improve, to leave the arrangement better than it found it; Sour has already stripped away what doesn't need to be there; Fried delivers the resulting attention with heat and immediacy, a decisive care that doesn't wait for permission to get the detail right. These Beans do not skim. They do not round. When they have finished something, the finishing is part of the thing.

The Fried Sour Cannellini Bean's challenge is the view from a distance. Meticulous care for the detail is a virtue — until the forest becomes invisible from this deep in the trees. These Beans must occasionally step back from what they are holding and ask whether the whole is moving in the right direction.
