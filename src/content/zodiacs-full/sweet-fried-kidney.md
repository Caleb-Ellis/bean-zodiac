Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fried Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most protective — the Kidney Bean's core instinct meets the decisive heat of the Fried Method, and what moves between them and the people they love is very little.

Sweet Fried Kidney Beans do not deliberate when someone needs cover. Kidney Bean already stands at the front of things when difficulty arrives; Sweet means the motivation is genuine care, not territorial instinct; Fried means the response is immediate, at full heat, committed before the question has finished forming. These Beans know they are going to show up — the only question is how fast, and it is always fast.

The Sweet Fried Kidney Bean's challenge is protection that leaves room for autonomy. Not every difficulty needs to be intercepted. These Beans must learn to ask whether someone needs cover, or whether they need to find their own way through — and to tolerate the answer either way.
