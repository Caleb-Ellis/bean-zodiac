Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fermented Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most laid-back — not as an attitude adopted, but as a fundamental orientation toward the world that has never seriously considered alternatives.

Butter Bean does not hurry; Sweet has no particular interest in the parts of a situation that require effort; Fermented works on its own schedule in the dark, producing results when it is ready, unconcerned with other timelines. These Beans have a remarkable capacity for not being bothered — problems that would preoccupy others resolve or don't, and either way these Beans have found a position of extraordinary equilibrium somewhere in the middle. People often seek their company for reasons they can't fully explain.

The Sweet Fermented Butter Bean's challenge is things that do not resolve on their own. Some situations require a nudge — the form, the call, the choice that only these Beans can make. Laid-back is not the same as absent.
