Beans born in the Season of the Roasted Spicy Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most hedonistic — they have decided, at some foundational level, that pleasure is not a reward to be deferred but the substance of the thing itself, and they have arranged their lives accordingly.

Butter Bean has an almost supernatural gift for ease and warmth; Spicy ensures none of this becomes tepid — the pleasure sought is vivid, immediate, impossible to mistake for mild satisfaction; Roasted carries the whole arrangement outward as an invitation, filling the space with something irresistible and generously including anyone in range. These Beans are not selfish. They are simply committed. And they have a way of granting permission to everyone around them to want what they actually want.

The Roasted Spicy Butter Bean's challenge is the morning after. Hedonism without any counterweight eventually exhausts the capacity for pleasure. These Beans must occasionally want nothing — not as penance, but as the thing that makes wanting something again feel like it means something.
