Beans born in the Season of the Fried Sweet Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most impractical — they are moved to help before they have worked out how, and they go ahead anyway.

Fried Sweet Mung Beans lead with the heart. Mung Bean's instinct is to tend and restore; Sweet makes this generous and immediate rather than measured; Fried removes any remaining hesitation, so the impulse to help becomes an action before it has fully become a plan. The results are not always what was needed. They are always genuine. These Beans arrive with exactly the wrong thing at exactly the right moment — and somehow it is still the right thing.

The Fried Sweet Mung Bean's challenge is diagnosis before treatment. Mung Bean at its best tends conditions; at its most impractical, it tends feelings and calls it done. These Beans must learn to ask what would actually help — before the oil is already hot.
