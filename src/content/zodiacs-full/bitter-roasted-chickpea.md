Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Roasted Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most outgoing — they move toward people as a matter of course, with a warmth that is genuine and a curiosity about who they are meeting that is, for these Beans, never quite satisfied.

Chickpea finds common ground wherever it lands — the zodiac's great connector, at ease in every room; Bitter adds a discrimination that keeps the outgoing quality from being indiscriminate — these Beans know the difference between interesting and merely present; Roasted carries everything outward with a conviviality that fills the space before the conversation has properly started. These Beans do not wait to be approached.

The Bitter Roasted Chickpea's challenge is solitude. Beans who move naturally toward people can lose track of what they think when they are not in the company of others. These Beans must occasionally sit with themselves long enough to hear what comes up.
