Beans born in the Season of the Sour Roasted Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most aloof — visible from across the room and genuinely difficult to reach.

Pinto Bean wears its story openly, but it has learned that this generosity is not always met with care; Sour has clarified exactly what happens when it isn't; Roasted fills the whole space with warmth that, in these Beans, manages to feel like warmth seen through glass — present, real, and not quite available. They are not performing distance. They are simply no longer making the first move.

The Sour Roasted Pinto Bean's challenge is habit. Aloofness that began as protection can calcify into posture. These Beans must ask themselves, occasionally, whether the wall is still load-bearing.
