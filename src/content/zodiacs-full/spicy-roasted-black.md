Beans born in the Season of the Roasted Spicy Black Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most magnetic — not through effort or strategy, but through the combination of depth and heat that makes something in the room orient toward them before anyone has decided to.

Black Bean darkens everything around it without announcing itself; Spicy means the effect is immediate and cannot be unfelt — there is no neutral reaction; Roasted fills the space with warmth that radiates outward from a source that doesn't need to move to be felt everywhere. These Beans do not try to draw people in. The drawing happens. Others find themselves held longer than they expected, returning after they meant to leave.

The Roasted Spicy Black Bean's challenge is intention. Magnetism without direction attracts everything equally. These Beans must decide what they want to draw in — otherwise the pull becomes indiscriminate, and they will find themselves surrounded by things they did not choose.
