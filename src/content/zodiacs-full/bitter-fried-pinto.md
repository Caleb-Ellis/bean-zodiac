Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fried Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most irreverent — they do not wait to see which way the wind is blowing before they say the thing.

Pinto Bean has always worn its markings as a kind of declaration, distinct and uninterested in blending; Bitter refuses to offer easy reverence to things that haven't genuinely earned it; Fried means this arrives fast, before the room has decided how to react. These Beans question the rule not out of chaos but out of a deep suspicion that most rules have not been examined in a very long time.

The Bitter Fried Pinto Bean's challenge is target selection. Irreverence aimed at pretension is useful; aimed at vulnerability, it's just unkind. These Beans must check their aim before they fire.
