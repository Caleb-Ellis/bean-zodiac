Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fried Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most headstrong — once a direction is set, they are already gone, and the conversation happening behind them is not a consultation they were planning to rejoin.

Green Bean does not wait; Spicy makes the chosen direction feel urgent and non-negotiable; Fried removes any remaining question about whether to proceed. These Beans do not require consensus. They have their read and they are moving on it. The force of it is often exactly what is needed — obstacles that would stop a more hesitant Bean do not stand up to this.

The Spicy Fried Green Bean's challenge is the room they have left behind. Headstrong works until it doesn't; the Beans who didn't get to weigh in sometimes have information these Beans needed. They must occasionally turn around before the destination, not after.
