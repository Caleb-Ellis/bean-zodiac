Beans born in the Season of the Fried Umami Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most pragmatic — they are not interested in whether a solution is elegant, only in whether it works, and they arrive at this position without guilt.

Butter Bean has no patience for unnecessary complication; Umami is focused on substance, on what is genuinely real rather than merely interesting; Fried delivers everything at heat, without deliberation, because these Beans have already done the thinking and arrived at the part where you simply act. They do not cling to method. They do not have a philosophy of approach. They have a problem, they have noticed something that will resolve it, and they are already doing that thing.

The Fried Umami Butter Bean's challenge is the unmeasurable. Not everything that matters can be settled by whether it works. These Beans must leave some room for the impractical — not as a concession, but as a genuine acknowledgment that some of the most important things resist the pragmatic standard entirely.
