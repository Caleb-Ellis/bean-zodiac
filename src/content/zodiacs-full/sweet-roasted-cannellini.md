Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most empowering — they have a talent for seeing what others could be and saying so with such warmth and specificity that the people around them start to believe it.

Cannellini Bean improves everything it is placed in — not by dominating, but by elevating; Sweet makes this freely given, without agenda; Roasted carries it outward into the room, filling the space with something that feels like permission to take up more space and try the larger thing. Being around these Beans has consequences — people leave them with new ideas about themselves.

The Sweet Roasted Cannellini Bean's challenge is their own ambition. Beans who are focused entirely on the growth of others sometimes neglect to want anything for themselves. These Beans must occasionally let someone else hold the lantern for them.
