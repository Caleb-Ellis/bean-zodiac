Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fermented Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most industrious — they are always working on something. Especially when it isn't visible.

Edamame turns everything toward use, nothing left over; Spicy provides the drive that makes work feel urgent, necessary; Fermented means much of it happens out of sight, over long stretches, in conditions no one else is monitoring. These Beans produce results that others cannot account for. The volume and the method are both their own.

The Spicy Fermented Edamame's challenge is legibility. Industriousness conducted entirely in private can look, from outside, like rest. These Beans must surface enough of the work-in-progress for the people around them to understand where they are.
