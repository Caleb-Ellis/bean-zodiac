Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fried Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most confrontational — they do not go around the difficult thing; they go directly toward it.

Bitter Fried Kidney Beans move fast and land hard. Kidney Bean is built to stand between the people it loves and whatever threatens them; Bitter strips away any comfortable softening and says what is actually true; Fried means the delivery is immediate, at full heat, with no time for the listener to prepare. These Beans are not cruel — but they are blunt in the way that only comes from caring too much to pretend.

The Bitter Fried Kidney Bean's challenge is knowing that not every difficulty is a threat to be met head-on. Some things open more easily from the side. These Beans must learn to distinguish between the confrontation that protects and the confrontation that simply escalates.
