Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most reserved — the warmth is present, considerable even, but it is not distributed at random.

Navy Bean is principled about what is given and to whom — loyalty is real, which is why it is not extended carelessly; Sweet means the reservation isn't coldness, it is warmth held carefully; Roasted fills the space with something gentle and unhurried, sustaining the temperature without performing it. These Beans are not withholding. They are waiting for it to be deserved.

The Sweet Roasted Navy Bean's challenge is the person who doesn't have time to wait. Not everyone will stay long enough to receive what is being held. These Beans must occasionally offer something before it has been fully earned.
