Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most curious — their interest in the world is warm, wide, and without agenda: they want to know because knowing is its own reward.

Green Bean notices everything and keeps moving; Umami means the noticing accumulates into something — depth built over time, pattern recognised across many different things; Roasted turns it outward, making the curiosity social and generous, directed at other people as readily as at ideas or objects. These Beans ask good questions and remember the answers. Being known by them is its own reward.

The Umami Roasted Green Bean's challenge is focus. Curiosity that goes everywhere can struggle to go deep in any one direction. These Beans must choose, sometimes, what is most worth understanding — not everything can be next.
