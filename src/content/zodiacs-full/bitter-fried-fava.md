Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Fried Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most fearless — not reckless, but genuinely unimpeded by the fear that stops most Beans before they begin.

Fava Bean has already decided the work is worth doing; Bitter has assessed the situation and found the risk a reasonable price; Fried converts that assessment into action immediately, before hesitation can find a foothold. These Beans are not fearless because nothing frightens them. They are fearless because they have learned that fear rarely has anything useful to add.

The Bitter Fried Fava Bean's challenge is that fearlessness can look, from outside, like a failure to take things seriously. These Beans must occasionally name what they assessed and overcame — not for themselves, but so others can see that this is not carelessness.
