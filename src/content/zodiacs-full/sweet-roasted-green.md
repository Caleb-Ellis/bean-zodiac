Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most capricious — they change direction with a warmth and conviction that makes each new enthusiasm feel inevitable, even as the last one recedes.

Green Bean cycles through possibilities fast, and nothing about this feels like failure; Sweet means each enthusiasm is genuinely and generously felt, not performed; Roasted gives it a heat and presence that draws others in before they've had a chance to question whether this particular direction will last. These Beans are not unreliable — they are responsive, and they would say that is a virtue.

The Sweet Roasted Green Bean's challenge is the Beans counting on them. Caprice experienced from the inside feels like aliveness; from the outside it can feel like instability. These Beans must communicate the change before others have built their plans around the previous position.
