Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fried Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most impulsive — the thought and the action arrive at the same moment, and both are gone before doubt could have found a seat.

Green Bean is already moving; Fried removes any remaining interval between decision and execution; Sour provides a cutting clarity that makes the first read usually correct. These Beans do not deliberate. They process by doing, and they are often right — not because they calculated, but because they absorbed enough context that their gut operates as an instrument.

The Sour Fried Green Bean's challenge is the impulse that was wrong. Sour's honesty does not protect against it; Fried's speed does not allow for correction mid-flight. These Beans must develop a capacity for a brief pause — not hesitation, just a breath.
