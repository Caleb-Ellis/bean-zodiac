Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fried Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most argumentative — they believe that getting to the truth is worth the friction of getting there.

Sour Fried Kidney Beans do not let things pass. Kidney Bean's tenacity means it does not drop a thing it has picked up; Sour has no patience for the comfortable version of a story; Fried means the response arrives at full heat before there is time to consider the temperature of the room. These Beans are not arguing for the sake of it — they are arguing because something was said that wasn't right, and they are constitutionally incapable of letting that stand.

The Sour Fried Kidney Bean's challenge is knowing the difference between the argument that gets somewhere and the argument that just keeps going. These Beans must occasionally ask whether winning the exchange serves the relationship — and whether it serves the truth.
