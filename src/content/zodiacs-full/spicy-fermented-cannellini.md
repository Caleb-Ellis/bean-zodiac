Beans born in the Season of the Fermented Spicy Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most persnickety — the small thing that isn't quite right bothers them, specifically, in a way they find difficult to set aside until it has been addressed.

Cannellini Bean curates ruthlessly — even a minor detail out of place registers as wrong in a way these Beans cannot simply overlook; Spicy means the irritation is immediate, a heat that doesn't cool just because the issue is technically minor; Fermented processes the whole thing internally and at length, which means by the time these Beans surface their concern, they have usually also arrived at the correct solution. The detail matters to them because the whole matters to them. The small thing is never just the small thing.

The Fermented Spicy Cannellini Bean's challenge is proportion. Not every small thing deserves the full weight of this machinery. These Beans must learn to distinguish the detail that is load-bearing from the detail that simply caught the light.
