Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Roasted Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most witty — the sharpness of the Edamame and the discernment of the Bitter don't close off the room. Roasted opens it, and the result is intelligence that lands as delight.

Edamame doesn't waste words; Bitter insists that whatever is said genuinely earns its place; Roasted means it arrives with warmth, filling the air rather than cutting it. These Beans can say the acute thing without making anyone feel small about it.

The Bitter Roasted Edamame's challenge is the temptation to use the wit to deflect. Sharpness that never lets anything land softly is still a form of distance. These Beans must occasionally let something be said plainly.
