Beans born in the Season of the Bitter Boiled Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most dogmatic — not in the small sense of stubbornness, but in the large sense: they have arrived at a framework for the world and they apply it fully, without exceptions.

Navy Bean already believes in systems, not because it was told to but because it has seen what holds and what doesn't; Bitter strips away anything that fails to earn its place with genuine merit; Boiled steeps the whole arrangement so slowly that by the time the conviction hardens, it has become invisible to them — indistinguishable from fact. These Beans do not argue from a position. They argue from the position.

The Bitter Boiled Navy Bean's challenge is the system that has outlived its evidence. A principle with no memory of its origin is no longer principled — it is just inherited. These Beans must occasionally ask not what the structure says, but whether the structure still knows what it was built for.
