Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most outspoken — they say the thing, clearly and warmly, and they don't particularly understand why more Beans don't.

Fava Bean has already done the work to know what it thinks; Sweet means what comes out is given generously, without edge — not a correction but an offering; Roasted fills the room with it, so the outspoken thing is warm rather than confrontational, the kind of statement that opens conversation rather than closing it. These Beans are not blunt. They are clear.

The Sweet Roasted Fava Bean's challenge is that not everyone receives forthrightness as a gift. These Beans must occasionally read the room before filling it. Some things land better when they have been cushioned by timing.
