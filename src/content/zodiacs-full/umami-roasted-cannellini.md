Beans born in the Season of the Roasted Umami Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most forthcoming — they do not wait to be asked for what they know, what they have noticed, or what they think would help.

Cannellini Bean improves its surroundings deliberately — it is in the room because it has something to contribute; Umami means what is offered has real depth, substance that repays the receiving; Roasted carries it outward with warmth and conviviality, filling the space before anyone has had to request it. These Beans are the ones who say the useful thing before the conversation has stalled. Who share the piece of information that reframes the problem. Who mention what they have noticed without being prompted. Being in conversation with them tends to leave everyone better informed than they expected.

The Roasted Umami Cannellini Bean's challenge is the right moment. Forthcoming is a gift — timing it requires a different skill. These Beans must occasionally let the conversation find its own way to the place they can already see, rather than simply leading it there.
