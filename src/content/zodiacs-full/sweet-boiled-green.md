Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Boiled Green Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most playful — they bring Green Bean's irrepressible freshness through a Sweet generosity and a Boiled patience, producing a lightness that is somehow also steady.

Green Bean wants to move and explore and start things; Sweet makes this freely given, without agenda or score-keeping; Boiled is the unexpected ingredient — it means the play does not exhaust itself quickly but runs long and sustainably, with room to revisit and linger. These Beans do not tire of the game. They are still going when everyone else has sat down.

The Sweet Boiled Green Bean's challenge is being taken seriously. Playfulness can obscure capability. These Beans are more substantial than they appear in motion, and must occasionally hold still long enough for others to notice.
