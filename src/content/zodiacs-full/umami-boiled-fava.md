Beans born in the Season of the Umami Boiled Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most vigilant — they maintain a sustained watchfulness that others let lapse, not from anxiety but from a deep understanding that the things worth protecting require consistent attention.

Fava Bean has learned that what is most valuable takes effort to access and effort to keep; Umami gives the vigilance its depth — not surface alertness but slow careful attention that notices things by accumulation rather than by flash; Boiled means it is sustained and consistent, without drama. These Beans don't miss things. Not because they are always looking, but because they never stop.

The Umami Boiled Fava Bean's challenge is the cost of sustained vigilance. Watchfulness maintained without rest eventually distorts the picture. These Beans must find a way to stand down occasionally — not to abandon the watch, but to stay accurate.
