Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Boiled Fava Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most unyielding — once they have decided something is worth doing, nothing rearranges that conviction. Not obstruction, not exhaustion, not the accumulated pressure of people who want them to stop.

Fava Bean is not deterred by the work — it moves toward difficulty as a matter of course; Spicy brings an intensity that makes the position urgent and impossible to dismiss; Boiled provides the patience to sustain that intensity for as long as the situation requires. These Beans don't wear out. They simply keep going.

The Spicy Boiled Fava Bean's challenge is the cost of being unmoved. Unyielding has the same shape from outside as immovable. These Beans must occasionally consider whether what they are holding firm against is the situation or the feedback.
