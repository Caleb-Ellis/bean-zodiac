Beans born in the Season of the Boiled Sour Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most trustworthy — they tell the truth, and they stay.

Boiled Sour Kidney Beans hold. Kidney Bean already holds its shape under long cooking, giving up its depth only to those who commit to the full time; Sour strips away any softening, any comfortable half-truth, and makes what remains more precise; Boiled ensures that none of this is hasty — the immersion is complete, the patience is real. These Beans colour everything they are part of, and because they have been honest about who they are from the beginning, the people around them know exactly what they are getting.

The Boiled Sour Kidney Bean's challenge is knowing when the pot is empty. Trustworthiness that never rests becomes a promise that cannot be kept. These Beans must learn to be as honest about their own limits as they are about everything else.
