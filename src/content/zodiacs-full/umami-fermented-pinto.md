Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fermented Pinto Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most apathetic — they have felt things so thoroughly that feeling has become, for now, exhausting.

Pinto Bean began as a vivid thing, its markings expressive by nature, its interior a landscape in constant motion; Umami has steeped this so deeply that the richness has become hard to distinguish from saturation; Fermented takes it all inward and works on it until it has become very quiet, very distant from where it started. These Beans are resting. They have earned it.

The Umami Fermented Pinto Bean's challenge is re-entry. Apathy is not neutrality — it is feeling that has gone quiet temporarily. These Beans must find the one thing that still moves them, and follow it back in.
