Beans born in the Season of the Umami Roasted Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most receptive — they take in what is offered with a completeness that is rare, registering not just the surface of what is said but what the saying contains.

Butter Bean creates calm without effort, the kind of stillness that makes other Beans want to stay; Umami adds a depth that rewards staying — a quality of genuine attentiveness that makes everything offered feel worth offering; Roasted brings warmth outward so that the whole thing is felt across the room, an invitation extended before any word is spoken. People speak more honestly around these Beans than they planned to. The combination of warmth and genuine interest turns out to be exactly the conditions under which truth arrives.

The Umami Roasted Butter Bean's challenge is their own expression. These Beans receive so well that they have made a kind of art of it, and sometimes the people around them forget that there is something to receive in return. These Beans must occasionally offer what they are holding.
