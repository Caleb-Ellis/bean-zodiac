Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Roasted Mung Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most easygoing — they move through difficulty without amplifying it, and they have a talent for making the situation feel more manageable simply by how they inhabit it.

Mung Bean accepts what is and works with it; Sweet makes this feel like generosity rather than resignation; Roasted fills the room with enough warmth that the temperature drops are less felt. Sweet Roasted Mung Beans are not passive. They are at ease, and ease is its own kind of active contribution — it gives everyone else permission to settle.

The Sweet Roasted Mung Bean's challenge is urgency. Not everything can wait. These Beans must occasionally let a situation be hard rather than immediately softening it.
