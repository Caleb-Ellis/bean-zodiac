Beans born in the Season of the Fried Sweet Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most nonchalant — things happen around and to them without making any visible dent, and they navigate situations that would consume others with a fluency that looks, from the outside, like not caring.

Butter Bean is constitutionally untroubled; Sweet adds a cheerfulness that makes nothing feel particularly heavy; Fried brings the heat and decisiveness, but these Beans deploy it without ceremony — they simply do the thing and move on. No analysis of whether it was right, no recrimination over what it cost. The oil was hot, the Bean went in, something was decided. The Fried Sweet Butter Bean carries none of the drama their method usually implies.

The Fried Sweet Butter Bean's challenge is seriousness. Not everything should be light. These Beans must notice when nonchalance is the right key and when the situation is asking for weight — and learn to pick up that weight without losing themselves in it.
