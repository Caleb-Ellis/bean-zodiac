Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Roasted Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most sociable — they arrive and the room becomes a better room, not through effort or charm deployed as a strategy, but through a genuine pleasure in people that is impossible to fake.

Chickpea has always been at home in any company — the zodiac's great connector; Spicy means the engagement is full-temperature, demanding real conversation, refusing the surface; Roasted carries the warmth outward until the whole room feels it, filling the space with a conviviality that makes people want to stay longer than they planned. These Beans do not need a reason to gather. They are the reason.

The Spicy Roasted Chickpea's challenge is depth over time. Sociability that keeps moving from room to room accumulates a wide warmth but rarely a deep one. These Beans must occasionally stay.
