Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Fermented Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most independent — they did not arrive at their conclusions by following anyone, and they are not planning to start.

Navy Bean already knows what the right thing is without being told; Spicy transforms what it touches, demanding full attention and generating its own momentum; Fermented completed the whole development out of sight, arriving at a set of convictions that no one else reached by the same route. These Beans are not contrarian. They are simply not following.

The Spicy Fermented Navy Bean's challenge is the isolation of self-direction. Independence is indispensable — until it becomes imperviousness. These Beans must remain open to the influence that doesn't threaten them.
