Beans born in the Season of the Fried Spicy Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most energetic — the Butter's usual stillness, run through Spicy's heat and Fried's refusal to wait, produces something that moves with a warm and total momentum.

Butter Bean's natural equanimity means the energy doesn't panic; Spicy ensures it cannot be ignored or directed anywhere trivial; Fried means it deploys at heat, without deliberation, filling every available space before the question of whether to fill it has been asked. These Beans move through the world at a frequency other Beans find either exciting or exhausting, sometimes both in the same afternoon. They are rarely still. They are also, somehow, never frantic.

The Fried Spicy Butter Bean's challenge is the aftermath. Energetic Beans leave consequences behind them. These Beans must occasionally turn around to see what has been left in their wake — not to slow down permanently, but to keep the heat aimed.
