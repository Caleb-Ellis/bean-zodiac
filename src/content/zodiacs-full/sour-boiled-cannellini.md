Beans born in the Season of the Boiled Sour Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most exacting — they hold a standard, and they hold it consistently, across contexts, over time, without softening when the occasion makes softening tempting.

Cannellini Bean is already particular about what it accompanies and how; Sour strips away sentiment, leaving only what is genuinely accurate; Boiled means the whole operation proceeds slowly and with total commitment — there is no rushing the Boiled Sour Cannellini to a conclusion it hasn't earned. These Beans do not lower the bar when the bar is inconvenient. They do not distinguish between the thing that matters and the thing that almost matters. The gap between adequate and correct is, for these Beans, the only gap worth discussing.

The Boiled Sour Cannellini Bean's challenge is other Beans. A standard that never bends eventually becomes a standard that only these Beans can meet. These Beans must decide when the requirement serves the work and when the work would be better served by letting it go.
