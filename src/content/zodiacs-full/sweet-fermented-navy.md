Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fermented Navy Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most introverted — they have a rich inner world and they live in it comfortably, without feeling that it requires exhibition.

Navy Bean is quietly self-sufficient — it does what is required without needing the room to notice; Sweet means the inner world is warm and generous, not bleak; Fermented does the whole development in private, building something substantial that doesn't require an audience to be complete. These Beans are not absent from the room. They are simply more present somewhere else.

The Sweet Fermented Navy Bean's challenge is the warmth that doesn't travel. An inner world that remains entirely interior is complete but not shared — and the people around these Beans may not know what they are missing. These Beans must occasionally bring the warmth out where it can do something.
