Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Boiled Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most compassionate — they have the patience to sit with difficulty, and the warmth to make it bearable.

Sweet Boiled Kidney Beans sustain the people around them. Kidney Bean's protectiveness extends naturally to those who are hurting; Sweet opens this into a generosity that asks for nothing in return; Boiled ensures that none of it is rushed — the presence is long and steady and real, not a brief warmth that passes. These Beans are the ones people think of when they are in difficulty, not because they will fix it, but because they will stay.

The Sweet Boiled Kidney Bean's challenge is receiving as well as giving. Compassion that runs in only one direction empties slowly. These Beans must allow the people they sustain to sustain them in return.
