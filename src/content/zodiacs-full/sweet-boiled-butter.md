Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Boiled Butter Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most calm — not the calm of distance, but the calm of a pot that has been on a low flame for a very long time and arrived somewhere genuinely, completely comfortable.

Butter Bean lowers the temperature of a room simply by being in it; Sweet extends this as a kind of generosity, an open-armed ease that invites everyone; Boiled brings the slow, patient depth that means none of this is temporary or performed. These Beans do not manage their serenity — they simply have it. Being around them is the atmospheric equivalent of something warm and nourishing at the end of a long day.

The Sweet Boiled Butter Bean's challenge is urgency. Not every situation should be lowered to their temperature. These Beans must learn to hear an alarm when one is sounding — not to become anxious, but to distinguish peace from obliviousness.
