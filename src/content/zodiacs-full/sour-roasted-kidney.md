Beans born in the Season of the Sour Roasted Kidney Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most fairness-minded — their warmth is not just generous; it insists on being distributed evenly.

Sour Roasted Kidney Beans have an instinct for who has been left out. Kidney Bean's protection extends naturally to others; Sour notices immediately when the distribution is off — when someone has been short-changed, when the arrangement benefits the wrong party; Roasted gives this not as cold critique but as genuine warmth, radiating the conviction that everyone in the room deserves the full welcome. These Beans make people feel seen because they actually looked.

The Sour Roasted Kidney Bean's challenge is carrying fairness as a value without carrying resentment as a companion. Not every imbalance is malice; not every correction needs heat. These Beans must extend their fairness to those getting it wrong, as well.
