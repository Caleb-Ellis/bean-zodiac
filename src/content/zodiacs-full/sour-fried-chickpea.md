Beans born in the Season of the Sour Fried Chickpea are the Bean Zodiac's most shrewd — they read people and situations with a speed and accuracy that can seem like luck, until you notice it keeps happening.

Chickpea has always had an ear for the room, moving between temperaments with fluency and understanding people quickly; Sour strips the situation down to what is actually going on, what is actually being wanted, what is actually at stake; Fried means the read is acted on immediately, before the moment passes. These Beans know the difference between what someone is saying and what they mean. They tend to see it first.

The Sour Fried Chickpea's challenge is generosity of interpretation. Shrewdness that reads everyone's motives eventually assumes the worst of them. These Beans must resist the temptation to be always right about what people are up to.
