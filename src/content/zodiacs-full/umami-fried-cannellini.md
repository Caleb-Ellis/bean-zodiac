Beans born in the Season of the Umami Fried Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most principled — they have a clear sense of what is right, and they act on it at heat, without waiting to see which way the room is going to move.

Cannellini Bean is a curator by instinct — it knows what belongs and has little patience for the version that compromises on the essential; Umami grounds this in substance, in the real rather than the merely convenient; Fried means the resulting conviction is acted on immediately, decisively, without revisiting. These Beans do not check their principles against the current consensus — they are consistent with themselves.

The Umami Fried Cannellini Bean's challenge is movement. Principles held correctly are a foundation — held too rigidly they become a cage. These Beans must allow that being principled and being open to revision are not mutually exclusive.
