Beans born in the Season of the Fried Umami Cannellini Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most principled — they have a clear sense of what is right, and they act on it at heat, without waiting to see which way the room is going to move.

Cannellini Bean is a curator by instinct — it knows what belongs and what doesn't, and it has little patience for the version of a thing that compromises on the essential; Umami grounds this in substance, in the real rather than the merely convenient; Fried means the resulting conviction is acted upon immediately, decisively, without revisiting, the oil already hot by the time the question has finished being asked. These Beans do not check their principles for consistency with the current consensus. They are consistent with themselves.

The Fried Umami Cannellini Bean's challenge is movement. Principles held correctly are a foundation — held too rigidly they become a cage. These Beans must allow that being principled and being open to revision are not mutually exclusive.
