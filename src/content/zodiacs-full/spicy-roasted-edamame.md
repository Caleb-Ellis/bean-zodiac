Beans born in the Season of the Spicy Roasted Edamame are the Bean Zodiac's most confident — not in the performed sense, but as a quality that radiates outward because there is nothing inward working against it.

Edamame has already taken stock, assessed what it has, and found it sufficient; Spicy means this self-knowledge carries heat, a presence that doesn't dim when questioned; Roasted fills the space with it — not aggressively, but inevitably, the way warmth fills a room. These Beans don't need to announce themselves.
