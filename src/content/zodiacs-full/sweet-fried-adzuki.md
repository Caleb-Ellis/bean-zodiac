Beans born in the Season of the Sweet Fried Adzuki Bean are the Bean Zodiac's most exuberant — the Adzuki's gift for celebration meets Sweet's uncomplicated generosity, and Fried gets to them before any of it can be measured or modulated.

Adzuki Bean knows when something deserves to be marked; Sweet keeps this welcoming and effortless; Fried means it is already happening before the decision has fully been made. They tint the moment the way adzuki tints the rice it's cooked with — before anyone has decided to let them.

The Sweet Fried Adzuki Bean's challenge is distinguishing celebration from distraction. The Adzuki Bean's shadow is avoidance, and exuberance is a particularly appealing form of it. These Beans must check whether the party is a response to life or a substitute for it.
