The Fava Bean is ancient — one of the oldest cultivated plants on earth, present in burial sites, sacred rites, and the diets of civilizations long dissolved. Those born under the Fava Bean carry this weight of history consciously. You feel the presence of those who came before you, and you understand that you are also an ancestor in the making.

You are not afraid of significance. Where others shy away from big questions, you move toward them: questions of legacy, meaning, mortality, and what we owe to the future. Your courage is not the flashy kind — it is the slow, deliberate bravery of those who carry on because someone has to.

The Fava Bean is also one of the most nutritionally dense legumes of all. You have more resources than you think. Draw on them.

**Fortune for this year:** Look to the lineage behind you, not for answers, but for strength. You come from people who endured. That is already a form of permission.
