The Butter Bean — also called the lima bean — is softness with substance. Creamy, rich, and deeply satisfying, it asks nothing of you except that you slow down enough to appreciate it. Those born under the Butter Bean are the great sustainers: the ones who ensure there is always enough, always warmth, always a place at the table.

You are not interested in spectacle. Your gifts are the kind that only reveal themselves over time — the patient accumulation of care, the steady show of presence, the home that always smells like something good is cooking. People seek you out not for excitement, but for restoration.

The Butter Bean reminds us that comfort is not a lesser virtue. The world runs on the quiet work of those who hold things together. Your year calls you to receive as generously as you give.

**Fortune for this year:** You have been giving steadily for some time. Let someone nourish you for a change. Abundance flows in both directions.
