The Edamame is the soybean caught in its most vivid moment: before the world has processed it into something else, still green and alive and bursting in its pod. Those born under the Edamame carry this same quality of bright, unrealized potential — an energy that draws people in and makes them believe that good things are coming.

You are at your best in motion. Ideas arrive quickly, enthusiasm comes easily, and the world seems full of open doors. Your social energy is contagious and your optimism is not naïve — it is strategic. You understand that possibility depends on showing up before the outcome is certain.

The edamame's challenge is finishing what it starts before the season turns. Potential unrealized is just potential. The Edamame year asks: what are you going to do with all that green energy?

**Fortune for this year:** The window for a particular opportunity will not stay open indefinitely. Begin now, with what you have, before the conditions are perfect.
