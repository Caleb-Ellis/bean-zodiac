The Lentil is the oldest bean in the room, and it shows. Cultivated for thousands of years, it needs no soaking, no fuss — just time and attention, and it gives back generously. Those born under the Lentil carry an old-soul quality that others recognize instinctively: a groundedness that comes from understanding how things actually work.

You are deeply practical. You have little patience for complexity that serves no purpose, for rules that have outlived their reasons, for performance over substance. You ask the right questions and you remember the answers. People come to you when they need to cut through noise and get to the truth.

The Lentil comes in many colors — green, brown, red, black — each a different kind of wisdom. Yours is specific to your experience. Do not try to generalize it into a philosophy. Let it remain particular.

**Fortune for this year:** Your instincts are sharper than you give them credit for. Stop second-guessing and act on what you already know.
