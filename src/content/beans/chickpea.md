The Chickpea, also called the garbanzo bean, is the great shapeshifter of the legume world. Whole or ground, roasted or braised, it transforms effortlessly across cuisines and cultures, yet always remains unmistakably itself. Those born under the Chickpea carry this same gift: the ability to fit any room without losing their essence.

Chickpeas are natural connectors. They bridge worlds, translate between temperaments, and have a rare talent for making people feel at ease. Their warmth is genuine and their curiosity about others is boundless. Where the Black Bean looks inward, the Chickpea looks outward — delighting in the texture and variety of all Beans and experiences.
