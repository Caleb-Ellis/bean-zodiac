The Black Bean is the keeper of secrets and the guardian of hidden truths. Like the fertile darkness of soil before the first sprout, those born under the Black Bean possess a profound inner world that few are invited to witness. Your strength is not worn on the outside — it lives in the quiet depths where ideas gestate and resolve hardens.

You observe before you act, weigh before you speak, and trust only what you have tested yourself. This deliberateness can be mistaken for aloofness, but it is simply the patience of something that knows its own worth. The Black Bean does not need to announce itself — it simply is.

In the kitchen of life, the Black Bean is foundational: unassuming, deeply nourishing, and capable of anchoring everything around it. You are the steady presence others return to when the world feels uncertain.

**Fortune for this year:** Look inward before looking outward. The answers you seek are already within you, waiting for the quiet to reveal themselves.
