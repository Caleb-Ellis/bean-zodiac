The Kidney Bean is built for endurance. Deep red and firm, it holds its shape under pressure, giving up its richness only to those who earn it through time and heat. Those born under the Kidney Bean carry a fierce and undeniable vitality — a life force that announces itself and refuses to be diminished.

You are a protector by nature. When those you love face difficulty, you are the first to stand between them and harm. Your passion is genuine and your loyalty unbreakable. You feel things fully — joy, anger, love — and you would have it no other way. Half-measures are not your style.

The Kidney Bean's gift is resilience: the capacity to be tested and come out richer, more flavorful for the experience. Difficulty is not your enemy. It is your forge.

**Fortune for this year:** Your strength is real, but even the strongest bean softens in good company. Let your guard down with the people who have earned it.
