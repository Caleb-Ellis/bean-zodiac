The Navy Bean is not glamorous, and it knows it. Small, white, and unassuming, it has quietly fed navies, families, and generations of those who needed something honest and filling. Those born under the Navy Bean are the backbone of every institution they join: reliable, consistent, and quietly indispensable.

You believe in the long game. While others chase novelty, you tend to what already works, investing in relationships and practices that compound over years. You find meaning in belonging — in being part of something larger than yourself, in honoring the traditions passed down to you.

Your challenge is the fear of being overlooked. The Navy Bean does not seek the spotlight, but it sometimes wonders if it should. Trust that the people worth impressing already know what you are worth.

**Fortune for this year:** The community you have quietly built is more valuable than you realize. Invest in it deliberately. The roots you tend now will hold you through any storm.
