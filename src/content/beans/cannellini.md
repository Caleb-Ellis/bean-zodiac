The Cannellini Bean is the aesthete of the legume world: ivory-white, smooth-skinned, with a texture so tender and a flavor so delicate that it elevates whatever it accompanies. Those born under the Cannellini have high standards — for their work, their environments, and their relationships — and they make no apology for it.

You are a curator by instinct. You edit ruthlessly: your home, your calendar, your circle. Not out of coldness, but because you understand that clarity requires the courage to remove what does not belong. Your presence tends to bring calm to a room — you have the rare quality of making things feel more considered.

The Cannellini's challenge is perfectionism. Refinement, taken too far, becomes paralysis. The Cannellini year asks you to release one beautiful, imperfect thing into the world rather than waiting until it is ready.

**Fortune for this year:** Done and shared is more powerful than perfect and private. Release something. You can always refine the next version.
